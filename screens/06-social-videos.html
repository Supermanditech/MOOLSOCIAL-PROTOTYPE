UX Verdict

Videos should first feel like a familiar video home feed, then become smarter than a normal watch product after tap. MoolSocial improves the model with verified proof, local/action context and one-tap next action without turning the feed into ads.

Primary Behavior

  • Open Videos into a feed with category chips, video cards and Shorts preview.
  • Tap a long-form video to enter watch mode; Back returns to the feed.
  • Show proof/action chips only when the video has a real product, service or work context.
  • Title and description stay collapsed by default.
  • Proof opens only when the video is commerce, booking or work-linked.
  • Mool and Chat remain visible without forcing role switching.

Creator Product

Long-form Videos supports deeper education, product demos, service explanation, professional proof, local business storytelling and creator monetisation without blocking consumer viewing.

Screen Contract What Screen 06 must preserve
EntryFrom Screen 04 Social > Videos or Screen 05 bottom Videos tab.
User lands in a Videos feed with topic chips, long-form cards and Shorts preview.
The active Social sub-action is Videos; tap a long-form card to enter watch mode.
PlayerAppears after tap.
Player supports play/pause, seek progress, HD/data state, cast, full-screen, captions and orientation handling.
When full-screen player opens, bottom dock can hide and return after tap/swipe, but Chat/Mool access must be recoverable.
DetailsCollapsed by default.
Title, views, time and creator are visible. Description, hashtags, source links and proof open in sheets or expanded sections.
This avoids the Screen 05 issue where text blocks video content.
ProofDecision-ready only when relevant.
Product/service/work-linked videos can show small verified chips in feed and a compact proof strip in watch mode.
Proof opens price, fulfilment time, trust proof, refund/cancel or payout rule. No endless product grid inside the video screen.
ActionsFamiliar video actions.
Like, comments, share, save, more/report, follow creator, search and recommendations.
Creator studio, monetisation and campaigns route later; they do not clutter the viewer screen.
BackendAPI-ready contract.
Needs video feed API, playback manifest, creator profile, engagement state, comments, recommendations and proof object.
Playback analytics, moderation, copyright/audio rights and creator monetisation events must be recorded server-side.
Full-Stack Implementation Notes For later production build

Video Data

  • video id, title, description, thumbnail, playback URL or manifest.
  • duration, language, captions, category, location relevance.
  • creator id, verification, workspace proof and follower state.

Engagement

  • like/save/share/comment actions with optimistic UI and reconciliation.
  • watch progress, resume position and recommendation feedback.
  • report, not interested, mute creator and safety controls.

Proof Object

  • price, delivery/fulfilment time, proof, cancellation/refund.
  • work videos can expose funded task, proof requirement and payout rule.
  • proof opens as a sheet or next flow, not as a permanent overlay.

Regression Tests

  • player visible above fold on mobile.
  • text does not cover video content.
  • Mool, Videos and Chat remain tappable.
  • sheets open and close without layout shift.
