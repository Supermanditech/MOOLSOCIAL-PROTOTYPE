Screen 46 handles salon service completion, payment and rating.

After the haircut or salon service is done, the app should not repeat booking or check-in. It should show the final bill, why paying through MoolSocial helps, rating, support and repeat booking.

Screen Contract Approved
EntryFrom Screen 45 when salon marks service done, customer taps service done, or payment approval is required.
User SeesCompleted service, final amount, payment status, app-payment reason, rating prompt, repeat booking and support chat.
DecisionUser approves/pays final bill, rates salon/stylist, books again, saves preference or opens support if bill/service is wrong.
BackendService completion event, final bill, payment status, card/UPI/cash record, rating, repeat preference and support thread APIs.
