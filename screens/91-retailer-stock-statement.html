Stock detail

Screen 91 is an auditable retailer Stock Statement.

It explains stock value, available-to-sell quantity, reservations, incoming goods and every stock movement. The live Stock catalogue remains the place to edit products and prices; this statement preserves source and balance truth.

Retailer wordingAll retailer-facing language uses Stock.
OpeningOpening stock comes from approved onboarding/import events.
ReceivedAccepted goods receipt increases stock exactly once.
SoldCompleted sale decreases physical stock.
ReservedAccepted orders reduce available-to-sell quantity, not physical stock.
ReturnInspected return becomes sellable or damaged stock.
AdjustmentManual count changes require reason, operator and audit history.
