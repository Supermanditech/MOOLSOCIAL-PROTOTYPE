UX Verdict

Feed should behave like a real social timeline: user scrolls first, taps into a post when interested, then reads or replies inside the post thread. MoolSocial improves this with verified decisions only on relevant posts, so social content does not become a noisy marketplace.

Primary Behavior

  • Users can post text, photo, poll, local update or proof-backed business/work content.
  • Tap a post to open full post detail; back returns to timeline.
  • Comment opens the same detail view and jumps into replies.
  • Feed filters remain social discovery controls, not role switches.
  • Relevant posts may show compact price/time/proof/refund/payout context.
  • Shorts and Videos remain one tap away through the bottom Social dock.
  • Mool and Chat remain fixed without blocking feed reading.

Creator and Business Product

Feed supports individual expression, creator reach, local business updates, service proof, work opportunities and community trust in one stream. It must not become an endless product grid.

Full-Stack Implementation Notes For later production build

Post Data

  • post id, author, body, media, language, location relevance and visibility.
  • post type: personal, creator, business, work, campaign or system update.
  • verification flags and workspace proof for business/work posts.

Ranking

  • For you, Following, Near you, Creators, Business and Work feed filters.
  • Freshness, trust, engagement, locality and abuse-quality scoring.
  • User controls: not interested, mute, block, report and hide.

Proof Object

  • price, fulfilment time, proof, refund/cancel and payout rule.
  • proof should be compact in feed and expanded only after tap.
  • backend must verify proof before order, booking or work acceptance.

Regression Tests

  • feed scrolls without bottom dock blocking actions.
  • text, media and decision strips fit on mobile.
  • Shorts/Videos/Feed navigation works.
  • sheets open and close without layout shift.
