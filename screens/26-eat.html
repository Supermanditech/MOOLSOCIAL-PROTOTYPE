Eat should solve hunger, table booking and daily meals without becoming a listing app.

Screen 26 starts the Eat main action with a restaurant chooser. The user can order from home, scan at a restaurant, pick a specific restaurant, use rewards, then choose Order Food, Book Table or Tiffin with price, fulfilment time, trust, cancellation/refund rule and the next action visible before order.

Screen Contract Approved
EntryFrom Universal Screen Eat, restaurant QR scan, specific restaurant search, social food post, chat recommendation or location prompt.
User SeesRestaurant chooser, delivery/QR/search/offers context, Order Food, Book Table and Tiffin routes with live summary and filters.
DecisionUser can choose restaurant, use loyalty, order now, pickup, book a table, start tiffin, scan QR or chat restaurant/support.
BackendRestaurant discovery, QR/table mapping, saved restaurant search, loyalty ledger, food catalog, availability, menu, prep time, table slots, tiffin plans, pricing, tax/fees and order status APIs.
TrustKitchen verification, live availability, loyalty applicability, cancellation window, final fee/tax, payment capture timing, refund rule and bill proof are visible.
FailureIf kitchen is closed, item unavailable, slot full or delivery unavailable, show replacement, next slot, pickup-only or alternate restaurant.
NextOrder Food routes to Screen 27 food menu/restaurant decision; Book Table routes to Screen 28; Tiffin routes to Screen 29.
LocationUses current area plus QR/shop presence when user scans at restaurant; does not assume permanent location without consent.
MVPStart with verified restaurants, tiffin providers and QR counter/table ordering in selected city clusters.
