Logo Type Wordmark-first identity

Master Name

Always write the brand as one joined word: MoolSocial. Do not split it as Mool Social in the logo.

Approved Lockup

White wordmark on navy field, saffron-white-green identity line, and white tagline pill with navy text.

Tagline

Use exactly: India Ka Socio Commerce App. Keep it in the pill for brand surfaces.

Typography

Use Inter or a close geometric sans fallback at 950 weight for the wordmark. Letter spacing must remain 0.

Shape System

Logo field and app surfaces use 8px radius. The tagline pill and identity line use full rounded ends.

Motion Rule

Motion is allowed only on digital splash or hero screens. Print, QR, legal, app icon and signboard outputs must use the static logo.

Usage Rules For all future screens and collateral

Do: use the navy-background primary logo for app splash, Play Store graphics, signboards, QR stickers, partner boards and major website sections.

Do: keep MoolSocial as one word, keep the tagline text exact, and keep the identity line below the wordmark.

Do not: use an MS monogram, M-only icon, separate external symbol, spectacles-style oo animation, Ashoka Chakra, national emblem, flag ratio, waving flag layout, or government-style approval signal.

Regression rule: future UI coding must use these tokens and logo proportions unless a new approved brand-spec file replaces this one.
