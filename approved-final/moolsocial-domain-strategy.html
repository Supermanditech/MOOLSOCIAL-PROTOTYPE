DNS And Security Setup Before public launch

Must Configure

  • Registrar account with 2FA and registrar lock.
  • DNS provider with change history and role-based access.
  • SSL/TLS on all public domains and subdomains.
  • SPF, DKIM and DMARC before sending email.
  • 301 redirects from defensive domains to moolsocial.com.

Do Not Do

  • Do not build separate sites on .in, .co.in, .social or .app.
  • Do not use multiple domains as separate brands.
  • Do not use a domain before trademark and compliance wording is reviewed.
  • Do not leave unused domains parked with third-party ads.
  • Do not publicly announce before buying the priority domains.
