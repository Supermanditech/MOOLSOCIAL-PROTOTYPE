Purchase order

Screen 83 is the authoritative wholesale order result.

Purchase-order identity, payment state, credit due date and delivery commitment are separated supplier-wise. A purchase order is not a GST tax invoice, and ordered quantity does not become retailer stock until goods receipt is accepted.

PO IdentityEach supplier-term group receives an immutable purchase-order number.
AdvancePaid amount keeps gateway, bank and MoolSocial payment references.
CreditCredit amount keeps its period, due date, limit source and settlement state.
InvoiceGST tax invoice is generated on the applicable supply or dispatch event, not PO creation.
StockInventory increases only after GOODS_ACCEPTED, never on ORDER_PLACED.
EventsConfirmation, dispatch, delivery and goods receipt are auditable events.
ExceptionsDelay, shortage, damage and invoice mismatch route to order support.
