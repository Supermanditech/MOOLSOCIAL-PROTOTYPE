Screen verdict

MoolSocial Shorts is a creator product, not only a content feed.

The user lands in vertical video consumption immediately. Creators get reach, follow, remix, save, audio/source, attribution and monetisation paths without turning the screen into a marketplace.

Try sample content

These samples show how Shorts can carry consumer, food and earning signals without becoming a marketplace grid.

UX lock

One vertical full-screen feed, one active short, minimal floating top controls, one right creator action rail, one thin bottom social nav. Mool and Chat stay reachable without covering the main content.

Creator product

Creator journey later expands into Create, Studio, Insights, Attribution, Campaigns and Payouts. On this screen, the visible creator hooks are follow, remix, save, original audio and contextual proof.

Screen Contract Entry, behavior and exit

Entry Condition

Screen 04 activeWorld is Social and active sub-action is Shorts. User has a valid individual session, area state and language state.

Primary Behavior

Load a vertical full-screen short-video feed instantly. Discovery modes exist in ranking/search, but the default viewer should not show a bulky header or category rail.

Exit Paths

Mool opens universal actions, Chat opens messages, Videos/Feed/Create route to their own social screens, and contextual cards route to the relevant world.

MoolSocial Shorts Product Scope Creator product baseline

Watch

Vertical swipe feed, For You, Following, Near You, Trending, search, captions, hashtags, local relevance, creator profile and original audio.

Engage

Follow, like, comments, share, save, remix, more/report, not interested, mute creator, block creator and safety moderation.

Create

Record, upload, trim, cover, captions, hashtags, location tag, original audio, remix permission, drafts and creator verification route.

Earn

Attribution, campaign eligibility, product/service proof, lead/order/booking signal, insights and payout route through verified workspace.

Competitor Parity And MoolSocial Edge User, creator and business view
User expectationYouTube, Instagram, Facebook pattern.
Instant vertical full-screen feed, swipe up for next, swipe down for previous, follow, like, comments, share, save, remix, original audio, captions, hashtags, report and not interested.
MoolSocial must match this expectation so users do not feel they are using a weaker social product.
Creator expectationGrowth and control.
Creator needs creation tools, drafts, audio, templates, remix permission, comments control, profile growth, insights, safety and monetisation path.
Screen 05 shows the viewer side; Screen 08 should become the creator creation/studio entry.
MoolSocial edgeSocio-commerce attribution.
A short can generate verified product demand, food orders, bookings, work tasks, local campaigns and creator-attributed outcomes.
The creator is not paid only for views; future payout logic should connect to verified demand, transactions, campaigns and trust.
UI/UX ruleNo clutter.
Advanced actions open in sheets. Top chrome fades when idle; context proof expands only on tap.
This keeps the experience familiar, cleaner and still gives MoolSocial its own operating-system layer.
UI/UX Rules Production-facing behavior
Content FocusVertical short-video feed.
Full screen visual content with only creator identity, follow state and one small Details affordance visible by default.
Caption, hashtags, product/service proof, earning proof and audio attribution stay hidden until the user asks.
Short NavigationBack and forth through shorts.
Mobile: swipe up opens next short; swipe down returns to previous short. Prototype also supports mouse wheel and ArrowUp/ArrowDown.
Navigation changes the active short only; it must not change root app world or bottom Social sub-action.
Discovery ModesCreator reach surfaces.
For You, Following, Near You and Trending are feed modes, but they should not permanently occupy video space in the MVP viewer.
Mode change can happen through search/filter/sheet later; the default view remains full-screen.
Right RailContent actions only.
Follow creator, like, comments, share, remix, save and more/report. Global Chat remains a separate pinned app action. Counts update optimistically, then reconcile with server state.
Right rail must not be used for root navigation because video actions need this space.
Context CardOnly when relevant.
A short may contain product, food, service, ride, booking or earning opportunity context.
That context opens through Details as a proof sheet for price/time/proof/cancellation or payout rule, so the video remains primary.
Creator ProfileOne tap from avatar/name.
Creator mini-sheet shows follow, message, profile, workspace proof, more shorts and safety controls.
This keeps creator discovery strong without putting a full profile card over every short.
Bottom DockMool, Social tabs and Chat.
Mool is fixed, Chat is fixed, Social sub-actions remain scrollable and readable: Shorts, Videos, Feed, Create.
Mool button shape is a shared brand anchor and must remain visually consistent across all focused sub-screens.
Creator ProductGrowth and monetisation path.
Shorts must support creator profile, follow graph, remix/original audio, content analytics, attribution, campaign eligibility, moderation and monetisation signals.
Payouts and creator campaigns are not shown as wallet UI here; they route to Work, Workspace or creator studio later.
Full-Stack Implementation Contract Deferred backend, defined needs

Required Data

  • shortId, creatorId, mediaUrl, posterUrl, duration and playback cursor.
  • creator name, verification state, creator type, location/category and follow state.
  • caption, hashtags, audio/source attribution, remix permission and moderation labels.
  • like/comment/share/remix/save/report counts and viewer action state.
  • feed mode, ranking reason, optional commerce/work context card with serviceability and proof.
  • creator eligibility state for studio, campaigns, insights and monetisation.

API Intent

  • GET /v1/social/shorts/feed for initial and paginated feed.
  • POST /v1/social/shorts/action for follow, like, save, share, remix, report, mute and not interested.
  • GET /v1/social/shorts/comments for comments and creator replies.
  • POST /v1/social/shorts/create for upload/record handoff from Screen 08 Create.
  • POST /v1/social/shorts/view-event for watch time and completion signals.
  • GET /v1/social/shorts/audio/:id for original audio and remix source.
  • GET /v1/context-card/:id for verified product/service/work attribution.

States To Design

  • Loading first short with skeleton poster.
  • Network loss while current short is already loaded.
  • No local shorts available, fallback to wider interest feed.
  • Removed or unsafe content state.
  • Creator blocked/muted, not interested, report flow and remix-disabled state.
Regression Gates Before production lock

No Marketplace Drift

Shorts cannot become a product listing screen. Product/service/work context must stay compact, verified and attached to the short.

No Navigation Conflict

Right rail remains content actions only. Root action switching stays with Mool and Screen 04 navigation logic.

No Role Switching

Creator, retailer, doctor, captain or manufacturer identity appears only as verified creator/business context, not as manual role tabs.

No Platform Copy

Use familiar short-video behavior, but keep MoolSocial's own layout, brand, creator attribution and socio-commerce proof system.
