People chat should stay familiar, private and useful.

Screen 25 covers personal and group conversations. It keeps normal chat first, then adds optional MoolSocial utility: shared basket, poll, media, invite and price-finding without forcing commerce into every message.

Screen Contract Draft
EntryFrom Chat Inbox People filter, personal notification, group invite, shared basket, poll alert or contact search.
User SeesPrivate people thread, member status, text, photo/video/file capability, voice note, shared basket, poll and quick replies.
DecisionUser can chat, call, video call, share media/file/location, edit basket, create poll, invite member, find price or move to Buy when ready.
BackendThread service links personal/group messages, media, basket share, poll state, invite state and optional buy handoff.
PrivacyPeople chats are members-only; business and support context appears only when user explicitly shares or routes it.
TrustCommerce actions are optional and transparent, so family chat does not feel like advertising or marketplace pressure.
NextBasket routes to Screen 09/10 buy flow; business quote routes to Screen 24; issue/order chats route to Screen 22.
FailureIf offline, show cached messages, queued sends, unsynced media and last successful sync time.
RealtimeRequires WebSocket/SSE, FCM push, message receipts, local draft cache, call signaling, poll sync and media/file upload queue.
