The retailer receives a paid order with clear preparation actions.

This screen appears automatically in the verified shop workspace after payment. Retailer operations never block the customer payment screen; exceptions become explicit substitute, reassignment or refund tasks.

Screen Contract Approved
EntryA customer completes payment and the paid order enters the retailer queue.
Alert SurfacePush notification, urgent in-app banner, Orders queue and smart role panel.
ActionRetailer starts packing, adjusts quantity, reports an unavailable item or updates ready time.
BackendReserve inventory when payment succeeds and create a preparation task for the retailer.
SLAIf preparation is not started, reassign the order or offer a customer-controlled substitute or refund.
TrustAccuracy and preparation performance improve future availability and fulfilment estimates.
NextStart Packing moves the retailer task to in progress and sends the customer a ready-time update.
No Role SwitchAppears automatically only for verified retailer workspace with pending action.
FallbackSMS can notify the retailer if the in-app preparation alert is not acknowledged.
