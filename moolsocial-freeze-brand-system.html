Brand Name

MoolSocial is the public brand. It must be written as one joined word in all UI, app listing, website and collateral.

Company Owner

SuperMandi Tech Pvt Ltd remains the company/legal owner. Public product identity is MoolSocial.

Category Line

Use exactly: India Ka Socio Commerce App. Do not replace it with marketplace, ecommerce, listing or directory wording.

Logo Type

Wordmark-first. No M icon, no MS monogram, no separate mascot/symbol, no animated spectacles-style oo treatment.

Core Promise

A direct operating socio-commerce super-app connecting social, consumer action, commerce, work, money and chat.

Primary User Rule

Every user is always an individual consumer first. Work, business and professional roles are added operating layers.

Locked Product Principles Carry into every future screen

What MoolSocial Is

  • Direct operating socio-commerce super-app.
  • Consumer-first with optional activated work/business layers.
  • One-tap access to social, commerce, work, money and chat.
  • Decision-ready services and product cards, not endless grids.
  • Search, ask, scan and voice as first-class entry points.

What MoolSocial Is Not

  • Not a marketplace.
  • Not yellow pages.
  • Not ecommerce listing.
  • Not a role-switching app with visible Personal/Retailer/Captain tabs.
  • Not a guaranteed-income or MLM-style promise.
