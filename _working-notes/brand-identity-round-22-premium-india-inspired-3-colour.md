## Legal-Safe Design Boundary

This design intentionally avoids:

- Indian National Flag layout
- three equal horizontal saffron/white/green bands
- Ashoka Chakra
- national emblem
- flag ratio or waving flag shape
- government approval signal
- tricolour badge or official-looking emblem

Use wording:

> India-inspired premium green, saffron and pearl palette.

Avoid wording:

> Indian flag logo / tricolour logo / national flag colours used as a mark.

## Legal Note

Not legal advice. Before trademark filing, confirm with an Indian trademark attorney. The design direction is safer because it uses altered India-inspired colour families and avoids flag imitation, but trademark examination can still be subjective if the mark is perceived as representing the Indian National Flag.

Official references checked:

- India Code: The Emblems and Names (Prevention of Improper Use) Act, 1950.
  - Section 3 restricts use of scheduled emblems/names or any colourable imitation for trade, business, trademark or design without permission.
  - The Schedule includes the Indian National Flag.
  - Source: `https://www.indiacode.nic.in/handle/123456789/1896?view_type=sea`
- Ministry of Home Affairs: FAQs about the Indian National Flag.
  - The National Flag is governed by the Flag Code of India, 2002 and the Prevention of Insults to National Honour Act, 1971.
  - The FAQ confirms the flag's rectangular 3:2 ratio and display rules.
  - Source: `https://www.mha.gov.in/sites/default/files/FAQ_18072023.pdf`
