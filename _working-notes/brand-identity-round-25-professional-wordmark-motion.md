## Request

Create a more professional looking animation from the full `MoolSocial` wordmark, after rejecting the earlier `oo` animation because it could look like spectacles or lenses.

## Direction

This round keeps the identity wordmark-first. The animation comes from the full name `MoolSocial`, not from an icon, not from `MS`, and not from the `oo` pair.

## Options In The Board

1. Signature Sweep - recommended.
2. Verified Reveal.
3. Action Rail.
4. Trust Pulse.
5. Market Converge.

## Recommendation

Option 1, Signature Sweep, is the strongest direction. It feels premium and professional because it keeps the whole name readable while allowing saffron and green energy to pass through the wordmark as a controlled motion.
