# Screen 04 License Gate Decision

Date: 2026-07-04

Decision:

MoolSocial UI/UX and full-stack development may proceed now for licensed or compliance-sensitive categories such as pharmacy/medicine, ride, doctor, health services and other regulated services.

This does not mean these services are approved for public launch.

Launch rule:

```text
Build now.
Hide or keep limited until compliant.
Public launch only after the required licence, verified partner, API/operator, local readiness and operating workflow are live.
```

Medicine/pharmacy rule:

```text
Medicine can be designed and developed in the Buy flow.
Medicine must not be shown as a live public ordering service until licensed pharmacy/vendor workflow is active.
Prescription medicine requires prescription handling, pharmacist verification, licensed pharmacy invoice, delivery proof and compliance record.
If the licensed workflow is not live in a service area, the consumer-facing Medicine action must be hidden or restricted to allowed non-drug wellness/personal-care products.
```

Reason:

The product architecture should be ready early, but MoolSocial must not create dead buttons, illegal fulfilment, fake serviceability or customer trust risk at launch.
