## Verdict

This logo direction is much closer and can be continued.

## What Works

- Horizontal lockup is compact and practical.
- Wordmark is stronger and more professional than earlier version.
- Deep teal/green feels trustworthy and premium.
- Small saffron dot is restrained and not flag-like.
- App icon crop is usable.
- Black/white version is considered, which is important for print and QR use.

## What Still Needs Improvement

1. The symbol is too close to a generic infinity / connection mark.
2. It may be confused with "CO" or a generic SaaS collaboration logo.
3. It does not yet express "Mool" strongly enough: source, root, value, origin.
4. The texture/gradient should be removed for final production logo.
5. The final mark should be a clean flat vector with fewer internal effects.
6. The saffron dot is good, but it should feel purposeful, not decorative.

## Direction To Continue

Keep:
- compact horizontal lockup
- deep teal primary colour
- tiny saffron accent
- Title Case wordmark: MoolSocial
- square app icon adaptation
- black/white version

Improve:
- make the icon more ownable
- reduce infinity-symbol similarity
- add subtle source/root/origin idea
- keep the symbol inline and no taller than wordmark height
