## Legal-Safe Composition Boundary

Because exact Indian flag colour values are being used, the design must strictly avoid:

- Indian National Flag layout
- three equal horizontal saffron/white/green bands
- Ashoka Chakra
- flag ratio or waving flag shape
- government emblem or official-looking seal
- any suggestion of Government of India endorsement

Use wording:

> India-inspired original-colour palette.

Avoid wording:

> Indian flag logo / national flag logo / tricolour trademark.
