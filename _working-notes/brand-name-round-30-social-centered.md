# Brand Name Round 30 - Social-Centered Names

Working note only. Not legal clearance.

## Goal

Apply all previous naming logic around **Social** as prefix, suffix, or meaning-root.

The name must not mean only:
- social media
- seller/vendor tool
- influencer app
- marketplace
- ecommerce listing
- B2B SaaS

For this business model, **social** means:

> people, trust, chat, commerce, work, payment, services and local economic action connected inside one operating app.

## Why SocialVend Is Weak

SocialVend means social selling. It is understandable to business people, but not ideal for this platform because:

- "Vend" is not everyday Indian public language.
- It sounds seller-first, not consumer-first.
- It does not clearly include money, work, chat, services, captains, creators or manufacturers.
- It can feel like a vendor tool rather than a direct socio-commerce super-app.

## Naming Filters From Previous Rounds

1. Must be easy for Indian public to say.
2. Must give some clue of what the app does.
3. Must not sound cheap or street-slang.
4. Must not become grocery-only, taxi-only, fintech-only or creator-only.
5. Must not force the user into seller identity.
6. Must feel premium mass India.
7. Must be usable in sentences:
   - "___ pe dekh le."
   - "___ se payment bhej."
   - "___ pe kaam mil gaya."
   - "___ pe customer mila."
   - "Mummy bhi ___ use kar legi."
8. Legal search comes only after emotional shortlist.

## A. Social Prefix Names

These are direct and clear, but some may feel too literal.

### 1. SocialMandi

Meaning:
- social + economic market/network

Why it fits:
- most understandable from first impression
- links naturally with SuperMandi
- can include buying, selling, work, local services and chat

Concern:
- may create marketplace/ecommerce perception if UI is not very clear

### 2. SocialSangam

Meaning:
- social confluence; people, commerce, money and work coming together

Why it fits:
- Indian, premium, inclusive, not seller-only
- strong social-commerce-work meaning

Concern:
- a little formal; needs modern design

### 3. SocialSetu

Meaning:
- social bridge between demand, supply, work, payment and trust

Why it fits:
- platform-like, broad, serious
- good for business and manufacturer layer

Concern:
- Setu may feel government/infrastructure-like

### 4. SocialSaath

Meaning:

Why it fits:

## Honest Recommendation

If the word **Social** must be visible in the app name:

> **SocialMandi** is strongest for mass understanding.

If the name should feel more premium and less literal:

> **SocialSangam** is stronger.

If we want to stay closest to SuperMandi's existing company identity:

> **MandiSangam** is strongest.

If we want a future-facing masterbrand that is not trapped in marketplace perception:

> **SangamOne** is strongest, but it needs a descriptor like "Buy, Work, Pay, Connect."
