## Verdict

Do not approve this version as final.

It is polished, but it sends the wrong category signal.

## What Works

- The wordmark is strong and professional.
- The colour palette is close: deep teal with restrained saffron.
- The app icon crop is usable.
- One-colour and black/white adaptations are shown.
- The brand system presentation is clearer than earlier versions.

## Main Problems

1. The symbol looks like a person/leaf/wellness/agriculture mark.
2. It may be perceived as organic food, NGO, health, farming, yoga or sustainability.
3. It does not strongly communicate socio-commerce, direct operating app, payments, work, commerce or digital trust.
4. The icon is too tall relative to the wordmark for tight app headers and QR stickers.
5. The tagline under the wordmark increases vertical space.
6. The green and saffron side bars near the tagline add unnecessary decoration and may feel tricolour-adjacent.
7. The bottom words "Source · Connect · Empower" introduce "empower" language, which can sound NGO/MLM-ish.
8. The period after "App." should be removed in final brand lockups.

## Keep

- Title Case wordmark: MoolSocial
- Deep teal primary colour
- Small saffron accent
- Poppins/SemiBold or similar geometric sans direction
- Square app icon adaptation
- One-colour and black/white versions

## Change

- Remove person/leaf form.
- Remove tagline from the tight primary logo lockup.
- Remove decorative side bars around tagline.
- Keep a compact horizontal logo for headers/QR/invoices.
- Create a separate full brand lockup only for posters or deck covers.

Make the main logo a tight horizontal wordmark with a small inline abstract mark. The mark should feel like a premium digital socio-commerce operating app: source/value, verified trust, connection and forward action. Keep the mark no taller than the MoolSocial word height. Use a flat vector style, deep teal primary colour and one tiny saffron accent only if purposeful. Remove texture, gradients, decorative bars and punctuation after the tagline.
