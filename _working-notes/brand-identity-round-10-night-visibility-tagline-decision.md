# Brand Identity Round 10 - Night Visibility And Tagline Decision

Working note only.

Brand:

> **MoolSocial**

Current category line:

> **India Ka Socio-Commerce App**

## User Issues

1. Current colour combinations may not be strongly visible on signboards at night.
2. Need decision on whether to use "India Ka Socio-Commerce App" or replace "App" with another word.

## Night Signboard Verdict

The current digital teal wordmark is not enough for night/offline signboards.

MoolSocial needs two colour modes:

### 1. Day / App / Website Mode

Use:

> Deep teal wordmark on white or very light surface

Reason:
- premium
- clean
- readable in app, web, invoice, Play Store, print

Recommended:
- Primary teal: `#043F3A`
- Night ink: `#071D22`
- White/day surface: `#FBFCFA`
- Accent saffron: `#FFB000` or `#FF9A1F`

### 2. Night / Signboard / Outdoor Mode

Use:

> White or warm-white MoolSocial wordmark on deep teal / night background

Reason:
- strongest visibility at night
- better for backlit boards, taxi stickers, shop signs, factory gates
- teal text on dark background is too low-contrast

Recommended night signboard:

- Background: `#052A2F` or `#071D22`
- Wordmark: `#FFFFFF` or warm white `#FFF8E8`
- Accent: saffron `#FFB000`
- Optional border/outline: white or reflective vinyl

Avoid at night:
- teal text on black/dark teal
- small orange text
- low contrast gradient
- thin strokes
- dark logo without backlight

## Tagline / Category Line Verdict

Use:

> **India Ka Socio-Commerce App**

Do not use **Superapp** publicly in MVP.

Do not use **Platform** as the main public line.

Do not use **Network** because it can create MLM/social-network confusion.

## Why "App" Is Best

**App** is the clearest word for normal users:
- consumer understands it
- auto captain understands it
- retailer understands it
- manufacturer understands it
- restaurant/service provider understands it
- creator understands it

Alternatives are weaker:

| Word | Issue |
|---|---|
| Superapp | overclaims MVP and may create expectation of everything fully mature |
| Platform | business/investor language, not mass public language |
| Network | can sound MLM/social network |
| System | too technical |
| Bazaar/Mandi | marketplace perception |
| Service | too narrow |

## Usage Rule

Primary logo:

> **MoolSocial**

Category line where space exists:

> **India Ka Socio-Commerce App**

Do not force the tagline into every small logo use.

Use tagline on:
- Play Store
- splash/onboarding
- website hero/header where space exists
- shop board/QR sticker
- investor deck
- public launch material

Do not use tagline inside:
- tiny app header
- favicon
- small QR corner
- small invoice logo

## Final Decision

Public MVP category line:

> **MoolSocial - India Ka Socio-Commerce App**

Night signboard rule:

> **White MoolSocial on deep night teal, with small saffron accent.**
