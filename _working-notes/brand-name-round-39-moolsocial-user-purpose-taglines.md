# Brand Name Round 39 - MoolSocial User-Purpose Taglines

Working note only. Not final brand lock.

Brand:

> **MoolSocial**

## Why "Daily Economy" Failed

Rejected line:

> The App For India's Daily Economy

Reason:
- "Economy" is too abstract for normal Indian users.
- A household consumer, auto rider, grocer, factory owner, restaurant or worker may not immediately feel "this is for me."

## New Tagline Rule

The tagline should make every user type feel included:

- consumer: I can buy/pay/book
- retailer/grocer: I can sell/get customers
- manufacturer: I can source/supply
- auto rider/captain: I can get work/payment
- restaurant/service provider: I can receive orders/bookings
- worker/professional: I can get verified work
- creator: I can connect, post, earn through real commerce later

Avoid:
- economy
- network marketing feeling
- earn/income promise
- vague platform words
- only social-media perception

## Tagline Options

### 1. Buy. Sell. Work. Pay.

Why:
- simplest and clearest
- consumer sees Buy/Pay
- retailer/manufacturer sees Sell
- captain/worker sees Work/Pay

Concern:
- does not mention Connect/Social explicitly

### 2. Buy. Sell. Work. Pay. Connect.

Why:
- covers full platform purpose
- adds social/chat layer

Concern:
- five words may feel slightly long

### 3. For Everyone Who Buys, Sells, Works And Pays

Why:
- directly connects every user type
- very clear first perception

Concern:
- long for small app icon/splash

### 4. Buy, Sell, Work And Pay In One App

Why:
- clear one-app promise
- no MLM feeling

Concern:
- "in one app" is functional, not emotional

### 5. Your App To Buy, Sell, Work And Pay

Why:
- personal and simple
- normal public can understand quickly

Concern:
- less premium than option 1

### 6. For Buyers, Sellers, Workers And Payments

Why:
- user-type inclusive
- factory/retailer/worker/consumer all see themselves

Concern:
- "payments" is not a user type; grammar slightly uneven

### 7. Daily Buying, Selling, Work And Payments

Why:
- plain, useful, no abstract economy language

Concern:
- a little descriptive

### 8. Where Buying, Selling, Work And Payments Meet

Why:
- communicates convergence without MLM

Concern:
- "meet" may feel softer

### 9. One App For Buying, Selling, Work And Payments

Why:
- very clear
- app-store friendly

Concern:
- generic but effective

### 10. Buy From People. Sell To People. Work With Trust.

Why:
- emotional, social and trust-led

Concern:
- longer; may sound like campaign line

### 11. From Source To Sale, Work To Payment

Why:
- strong for manufacturer, retailer, worker, commerce

Concern:
- consumer may not immediately feel included

### 12. Buy, Sell, Serve And Pay

Why:
- includes service providers
- clean action words

Concern:
- work/captain layer less explicit

### 13. Buy, Sell, Serve, Work, Pay

Why:
- very inclusive

Concern:
- too many verbs

### 14. India Buys, Sells, Works And Pays Here

Why:
- big public feeling
- every user type included

Concern:
- "here" may imply marketplace/place

### 15. For Shops, Homes, Work And Payments

Why:
- normal people understand instantly
- household + retailer + worker + money

Concern:
- manufacturer and creator not explicit

## Best Shortlist

### Best Overall

> **Buy. Sell. Work. Pay.**

Why:
- shortest
- clearest
- premium enough
- each core user type sees their purpose

### Best Complete Platform Line

> **Buy. Sell. Work. Pay. Connect.**

Why:
- includes social/chat
- complete SuperMandi/MoolSocial purpose

### Best Public Explanation

> **For Everyone Who Buys, Sells, Works And Pays**

Why:
- normal public understands
- not abstract
- inclusive across user types

### Best App Store Line

> **One App For Buying, Selling, Work And Payments**

Why:
- clear and safe
- no overpromise

## Recommendation

Use this as the main tagline:

> **Buy. Sell. Work. Pay.**

Use this as the explanatory line where more space exists:

> **For Everyone Who Buys, Sells, Works And Pays**

Brand display:

> **MoolSocial**  
> **Buy. Sell. Work. Pay.**

Long public-board/app-store version:

> **MoolSocial - For Everyone Who Buys, Sells, Works And Pays**
