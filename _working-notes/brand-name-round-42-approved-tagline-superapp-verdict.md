# Brand Name Round 42 - Approved Tagline And Superapp Verdict

Working note only.

Brand:

> **MoolSocial**

Approved tagline direction from user:

> **India Ka Socio-Commerce Superapp**

## Verdict On "Superapp"

Do not use **Superapp** as the main public MVP tagline yet.

Use:

> **India Ka Socio-Commerce App**

Reason:
- "Superapp" is a big promise. If every module is not fully ready on day one, users may feel the claim is inflated.
- Normal public understands "app" faster than "superapp."
- Investors and tech audiences understand "superapp," but household users, captains, grocers, workers and factory users respond better to direct clarity.
- "Socio-Commerce" already gives the category. Adding "superapp" can make the line heavier.

## Recommended Use By Context

### Public / Play Store / First Screen

> **MoolSocial**  
> **India Ka Socio-Commerce App**

### Pitch Deck / Investor / Long Brand Description

> **MoolSocial is India Ka Socio-Commerce Superapp**

### Future Full-Scale Version

Use **Superapp** publicly after the app genuinely has multiple mature, reliable layers live:
- social
- commerce
- services
- work
- money/payment
- chat/support
- business/professional role layers

## Final Recommendation

Primary MVP tagline:

> **India Ka Socio-Commerce App**

Strategic category line:

> **India Ka Socio-Commerce Superapp**
