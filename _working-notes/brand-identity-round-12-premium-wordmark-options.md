## User Feedback

The previous round was readable, but did not have enough richness or premiumness.

## New Direction

Move away from sticker-style utility and bright underlines. Build a more premium public-trust identity with:
- Full wordmark-first identity.
- No standalone icon.
- No M-only mark.
- No MS shortcut.
- No split Mool/Social identity.
- Deeper emerald, charcoal, warm ivory and restrained champagne gold.
- Day and night lockups for app, website, print, taxi/shop boards and QR stickers.
