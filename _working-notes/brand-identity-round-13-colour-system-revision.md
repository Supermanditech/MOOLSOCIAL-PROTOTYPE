# MoolSocial Brand Identity - Round 13 Colour System Revision

Date: 2026-06-26

## User Feedback

The premium wordmark structures are acceptable, but the colour combination needs to change.

## Decision

Keep the same five wordmark-first logo options and revise only the colour system.

## New Colour System

- Midnight Navy: `#0B3A53`
  - Main day wordmark colour.
  - More premium and international than the previous dark emerald.

- Night Ink: `#06131F`
  - Outdoor/night signboard base.
  - Stronger for night visibility.

- Warm Ivory: `#FFF9ED`
  - Night wordmark and premium light surface.
  - Better than pure white for richness while still readable.

- Champagne Gold: `#D8A23A`
  - Restrained memory/accent colour.
  - Used as a signal, not decoration.

- Clean Teal: `#007C78`
  - App/social freshness and modernity.
  - Supports the socio-commerce side without making the logo look like a grocery-only brand.
