# Brand Name Round 38 - MoolSocial Premium Taglines

Working note only. Not final brand lock.

Brand:

> **MoolSocial**

## Why Previous Emotional Line Failed

Rejected line:

> Jahan Log Judte Hain, Kaam Hota Hai

Reason:
- Can feel like MLM/network marketing.
- Too much emphasis on people joining.
- Not premium enough.
- Does not sound like a serious consumer-tech / commerce / operating platform.

## New Tagline Criteria

The tagline must:
- sound premium and trustworthy
- avoid MLM/network-marketing language
- avoid "join", "earn", "network banaao", "log jodo" style
- not overpromise income
- not sound like a social media app only
- clearly imply utility, commerce, payment, work and trust
- work on Play Store, grocery board, taxi sticker, factory board and investor deck

### Best Simple Public Line
