# Brand Name Round 10 - Aarya + Vivaan Origin

User direction:

All previous round names are rejected. Look for something possible from Aarya and Vivaan, the user's daughter and son.

This round uses a new parameter:

The name should carry a private founder-origin story from Aarya + Vivaan, but should not look like a child's name only. It must still feel like a serious national operating super-app.

## Important Naming Rule

Do not use exact `Aarya` or `Vivaan` as the consumer app name.

Reasons:

- too common as personal names
- weaker trademark distinctiveness
- too visibly personal
- may not feel like a national operating platform

Better strategy:

Use hidden-origin names built from:

- `Aar` / `Arya` from Aarya
- `Vi` / `Vaan` from Vivaan
- blended forms that are easy to pronounce and strong as a brand

## Quick Surface Legal Signals

This is not final legal clearance, but public web scan shows caution:

- `AARVA` appears in IndiaFilings trademark records in class 25 and `AARVA CARE` in class 10.
- `AVAAN` appears in IndiaFilings trademark records, including class 35.
- `VARYA` appears in IndiaFilings records, including class 35, and also appears as a current India AI/video model name in 2026 media.
- `AARVYA` appears in recent IndiaFilings records for classes 29 and 35.
- `AAVYA` appears in IndiaFilings records, including class 25 and abandoned class 35 applications.

These do not automatically kill all related names, but they mean the final choice must be checked carefully with an IP lawyer and official IP India search.
