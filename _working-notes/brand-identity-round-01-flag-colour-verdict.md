# Brand Identity Round 01 - Indian Flag Colour Verdict

Working note only. Not legal opinion.

Brand:

> **MoolSocial**

Tagline:

> **India Ka Socio-Commerce App**

## Question

Can MoolSocial use Indian flag colours in the logo?

## Verdict

Do **not** use the Indian National Flag or a flag-like tricolour logo.

Use **India-inspired colours** only as subtle accents.

## Safe Direction

Allowed direction:
- saffron-inspired accent
- green-inspired accent
- white/neutral background
- blue or teal base identity
- colours used in abstract shapes, gradients, dots, routes, checkmarks, rings or UI accents

Avoid:
- exact Indian flag layout
- three equal horizontal bands of saffron, white and green
- Ashoka Chakra or chakra-like wheel in the center
- flag ratio or waving flag shape
- placing MoolSocial text over a flag
- using national emblem, Ashoka lions or government-style symbols
- any design that suggests Government of India approval

## Recommended Logo Direction

Primary brand colour:

> Deep trust green / teal

Accent colours:

> Warm saffron accent + fresh green accent

Design idea:

> MoolSocial logo should look like a socio-commerce operating app, not a patriotic/government app.

Possible visual language:
- MS monogram
- connected root/node symbol
- source-to-people loop
- trust/check mark
- chat + commerce signal
- rounded app icon with premium India-inspired accents

## Legal Basis To Review

The Emblems and Names (Prevention of Improper Use) Act, 1950 restricts commercial/trademark use of the Indian National Flag or colourable imitation without permission.

The Schedule includes:

> The Indian National Flag

Official source:

https://upload.indiacode.nic.in/view-casepdf?id=AC_CEN_21_44_00002_195012_1517807320273&type=act

The Flag Code of India governs display/use of the national flag:

https://www.mha.gov.in/sites/default/files/flagcodeofindia_070214.pdf

## Practical Brand Recommendation

Use:

> **Indian feeling, not Indian flag.**

For MoolSocial:

> A premium teal/green masterbrand with small saffron and green accents is safer and more professional than a tricolour flag-style logo.
