# Brand Name Round 12 - Aarya + Vivaan Neutral Letters

User direction:

Aarya and Vivaan are Hindu names. The app name should be loveable to all religions, but should still keep letters or characters from the children's names so they feel included.

Parked fallback names:

- Yonda
- Omera

## New Naming Rule

Do not use Aarya or Vivaan directly.

Do not make the name sound religious, caste-coded, Sanskrit-heavy, or like only a baby name.

Use a hidden family signature:

- `A` from Aarya
- `V` / `Vi` / `Van` from Vivaan
- optional `R`, `Y`, `N` characters from the two names

The name should feel secular, modern, pan-India and platform-grade.

## 10 Brand Name Options

1. **Avora**
   - Source letters: A + V from Aarya and Vivaan.
   - Why it fits: broad, modern, emotionally warmer than Yonda, not religious-sounding.
   - Extension: Avora Pay, Avora Work, Avora Business, Avora Creator.

2. **Avero**
   - Source letters: A + V + R from the children names.
   - Why it fits: strong, simple, app-like, less soft than Omera.
   - Extension: Avero Pay, Avero Captain, Avero Business.

3. **Avarin**
   - Source letters: A + V + A + R + I + N.
   - Why it fits: serious enough for business/manufacturer, still friendly for consumer.
   - Extension: Avarin Work, Avarin Pay, Avarin Creator.

4. **Navira**
   - Source letters: N + A + V + I + R + A.
   - Why it fits: more emotional and memorable, not obviously religious.
   - Extension: Navira Social, Navira Pay, Navira Business.

5. **Rivana**
   - Source letters: R + I + V + A + N + A.
   - Why it fits: smooth, loveable, social-friendly, consumer-friendly.
   - Extension: Rivana Creator, Rivana Pay, Rivana Work.

6. **Avian**
   - Source letters: A + V + I + A + N.
   - Why it fits: short, global, clean.
   - Risk: may sound too close to aviation/avian associations.

7. **Avina**
   - Source letters: A + V + I + N + A.
   - Why it fits: simple, warm, easy to say.
   - Risk: may feel like a person name more than a platform.

8. **Arvona**
   - Source letters: A + R + V + A + N, with neutral brand sound.
   - Why it fits: stronger and more platform-like than softer names.
   - Extension: Arvona Business, Arvona Pay, Arvona Work.

9. **Viana**
   - Source letters: V + I + A + N + A.
   - Why it fits: very easy to say, peaceful and broad.
   - Risk: softer and more name-like.

10. **Avaya**
    - Source letters: A + V + A + Y + A.
    - Why it fits: beautiful, simple, all-religion friendly sound.
    - Risk: major existing global telecom brand; likely not practical legally.

## Best 3 From This Round

1. **Avora**
2. **Avero**
3. **Avarin**

## Current Recommendation

**Avora**

Reason:

It keeps the hidden `A + V` family signature from Aarya and Vivaan, but does not sound like a Hindu name. It is loveable, modern, easy to pronounce and broad enough for consumer, social, creator, captain, business, manufacturer, money and work layers.

## Possible Logo Direction

Use `A` and `V` as the hidden emotional mark:

- App name: Avora
- Icon mark: interlocked A/V
- Story for children: "Aarya and Vivaan are inside the first two letters."
- Public story: "Avora is India's daily operating app for people, work, money and local life."
