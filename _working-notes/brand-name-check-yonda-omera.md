# Brand Name Check - Yonda / Omera

Date: 2026-06-25

Purpose:

Public risk screen for the parked names:

- Yonda
- Omera

Scope:

- Public web search
- Public trademark directory visibility
- MCA-style public company directory visibility
- App Store / Play Store visibility
- Category conflict risk

This is not final legal clearance. Final clearance must be done through official IP India public search, official MCA portal search, app-store searches, domain/handle scans and trademark counsel review.

## Result Summary

| Name | Risk View | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| Yonda | Amber / High caution | Reject |
| Omera | Red | Reject |

## Yonda Findings

### India Company / MCA-Style Directory

Public company directories show:

- `YONDA SKILLS PRIVATE LIMITED`
- CIN: `U74995KA2018PTC111596`
- RoC: Bangalore
- Status: Active
- Incorporated: 23-03-2018

Risk:

This does not automatically block a consumer app brand, but the exact word `YONDA` already exists in an active Indian private company name.

### App / Product Visibility

Visible app/product use includes:

- `YondaApp` on Google Play by `Yonda, MCHJ`
- `Yonda QR` on Google Play
- `Yonda QR` on Apple App Store
- `Yonda, MCHJ` developer page with multiple apps

Risk:

The exact name `Yonda` is already used as an app/developer identity. This is not India-specific only, but it weakens uniqueness for a super-app.

### Business / Fintech / Tax Visibility

Visible business use includes:

- `Yonda Tax`
- `Yonda Sales Tax Automation`
- `Yondaa` legaltech/company formation/trademark service
- Older `Yonda` prepaid/gift-card app by Blackhawk Network, where the source says `Yonda` was a registered trademark.

Risk:

This matters because SuperMandi has money, commerce, business, tax/GST-facing merchant, payments and operational layers. The name is not clean in business/finance/compliance-adjacent areas.

### Yonda Verdict

Reject Yonda.

Reason:

It is emotionally not strong enough, and the public-risk screen is not clean enough to justify keeping it. Visible conflicts include an active Indian company name, app-store usage, business/tax/finance-adjacent usage, and close variant usage.

Even if official IP India and MCA checks later looked manageable, it should not stay in the shortlist because the brand objective needs a cleaner, stronger, more instantly ownable national name.

Earlier fallback condition now removed:

- official IP India exact/phonetic search is acceptable
- class 9/35/36/38/39/41/42/43/45 conflicts are manageable
- MCA name conflict is acceptable to counsel
- app store confusion risk is acceptable

Current recommendation:

Do not keep Yonda.

## Omera Findings

### Trademark Visibility

Public trademark directories show visible `OMERA` records, including:

- `OMERA`, class 9, status registered
- application number `342229`
- description includes apparatus/instruments/accessories for electronics, telecommunications, radio, TV, telephony, etc.
- proprietor listed as `SOCIETE D'OPTIQUE DE MACANIQUE`

Other `OMERA` trademark records are also visible:

- class 5 withdrawn
- class 99 expired, with class references including 7 and 5
- a newer `Omera` device mark is visible on QuickCompany

Risk:

Class 9 is directly sensitive because apps, software and electronic/digital goods often sit around class 9. For SuperMandi, this is a serious warning.

### India Company / MCA-Style Directory

Public company directories show many Omera companies, including:

- `OMERA TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED`
- `OMERA ELECTRONICS PRIVATE LIMITED`
- `OMERA SOLUTIONS (OPC) PRIVATE LIMITED`
- `OMERA TRADING PRIVATE LIMITED`
- `OMERA INFRAVENTURES PRIVATE LIMITED`
- `OMERA GROUP INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED`
- `OMERA CONSTRUCTION MULTI SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED`
- `OMERA HEALTHCARE PRIVATE LIMITED`
- others

Risk:

The name is already common enough in Indian company records. It does not feel ownable for a national super-app.

### App / AI / Technology Visibility

Visible app and technology use includes:

- `Omera: AI Writing Keyboard` on Apple App Store, including India listing
- `Omera: Your AI Keyboard` website
- `OMERA AI Solutions`, Pune, India, offering AI automation for customer communication, bookings, documents and workflows
- `OMERA` Android app in auto/vehicle/work activity context

Risk:

This is close to SuperMandi's AI, chat, business automation and workflow layers.

### Global / Category Visibility

Visible broader use includes:

- Omera LPG / Omera Petroleum in Bangladesh
- Omera apps by Omera Petroleum Limited on Google Play
- Omera restaurants/hospitality references
- Omera as a personal name

Risk:

High public noise and category spread.

### Omera Verdict

Reject Omera.

Reason:

It has visible registered class 9 trademark risk, many India company-name collisions, existing AI/app usage, and broader public noise. It is too crowded for a national super-app.

## Final Recommendation

Drop Omera.

Drop Yonda.

The next naming direction should keep only what almost worked in Yonda:

- simple
- neutral
- platform-capable
- easy enough to say

But improve:

- first-impression meaning
- public conflict cleanliness
- India mass recall
- legal ownability
