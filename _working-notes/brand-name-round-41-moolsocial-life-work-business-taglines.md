# Brand Name Round 41 - MoolSocial Life / Work / Business Taglines

Working note only. Not final brand lock.

Brand:

> **MoolSocial**

## Why Previous Direction Failed

Rejected direction:

> Har Zarurat Ka Sahi Mool

Reason:
- Too Hindi-copywriting.
- Too price/value-heavy.
- Can feel like grocery/discount positioning.
- Does not feel like a premium national app line.

## New Direction

Avoid:
- economy
- network / log jodo
- earn / opportunity / MLM smell
- feature list like buy/sell/work/pay
- Hindi slogan feeling
- price-first meaning

Use:
- life
- work
- business
- daily India
- professional simplicity
- one app but not marketplace

The line must work for:
- household consumer
- auto captain
- grocer / retailer
- manufacturer / factory
- restaurant
- service provider
- worker / professional
- creator

## Tagline Options

### 1. Built For The Way India Lives And Works

Why:
- premium and national
- includes household + worker + business naturally
- no MLM / no feature list

Concern:
- commerce/payment not explicit, but app visuals can explain.

### 2. For India’s Everyday Life And Work

Why:
- simple and public-friendly
- household and work users both included

Concern:
- less business/manufacturer explicit.

### 3. Life, Work And Business In One App

Why:
- very clear
- household, worker, shop and factory all included

Concern:
- slightly functional.

### 4. For Homes, Work And Business

Why:
- every main user type can see themselves
- simple enough for public boards

Concern:
- not emotionally rich.

### 5. Where Daily Life Meets Daily Work

Why:
- premium and human
- avoids marketplace and MLM feeling

Concern:
- business/manufacturer less explicit.

### 6. India’s App For Life, Work And Business

Why:
- strong app-category line
- clear and serious

Concern:
- slightly long.

### 7. Made For Homes, Shops And Work

Why:
- normal Indian public understands instantly
- consumer, retailer and worker visible

Concern:
- factory/manufacturer not explicit.

### 8. For Every Home, Shop And Workplace

Why:
- public-board friendly
- includes retailer/factory/office/worker

Concern:
- service and creator not explicit but can fit.

### 9. India’s Daily App For Home, Work And Business

Why:
- clear
- broad
- no technical language

Concern:
- a little descriptive.

### 10. From Home To Work To Business

Why:
- journey-like and memorable
- includes all daily contexts

Concern:
- not explicit about action.

### 11. One App For Home, Work And Business

Why:
- extremely clear
- mass understandable

Concern:
- common style.

### 12. Built For India’s Homes, Shops And Workplaces

Why:
- very clear for public boards
- retailer/manufacturer/workers included

Concern:
- longer but strong.

## Best Shortlist

### Best Overall

> **Built For The Way India Lives And Works**

Why:
- premium
- inclusive
- emotional but not filmy
- broad enough for all roles
- avoids MLM/marketplace/feature-list problems

### Best Clear Public Line

> **One App For Home, Work And Business**

Why:
- most understandable by normal users
- every major user type sees a place for themselves

### Best Board / Sticker Line

> **For Every Home, Shop And Workplace**

Why:
- works on grocery shop, taxi partner board, factory gate, service provider display

### Best Launch Descriptor

> **India’s App For Life, Work And Business**

Why:
- app-store friendly
- serious and national

## Recommendation

Primary tagline:

> **MoolSocial - Built For The Way India Lives And Works**

Simpler public version:

> **MoolSocial - One App For Home, Work And Business**

Board/sticker version:

> **MoolSocial - For Every Home, Shop And Workplace**
