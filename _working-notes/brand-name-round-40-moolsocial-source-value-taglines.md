# Brand Name Round 40 - MoolSocial Source/Value Taglines

Working note only. Not final brand lock.

Brand:

> **MoolSocial**

## Why Previous Line Failed

Rejected:

> Buy. Sell. Work. Pay.

Reason:
- Too mechanical.
- Feels like a feature list, not a brand line.
- Does not create memory or emotion.
- Does not feel premium enough for a serious India-scale app.

## New Direction

No:
- economy
- network
- join / jodo people
- earning promise
- feature checklist
- marketplace wording
- social-media-only wording

Yes:
- need
- source
- value
- trust
- daily use
- right place / right action / right outcome
- user-type neutral but meaningful

The tagline should make each user feel:

- consumer: my need gets fulfilled
- retailer/grocer: my product/customer purpose fits
- manufacturer: source/value-chain purpose fits
- auto captain/worker: my work/service has value
- restaurant/service provider: my service can reach people
- creator: my social presence can connect to real value later

## New Tagline Options

### 1. Har Zarurat Ka Sahi Mool

Meaning:
- the right source/value for every need

Why it works:
- uses Mool naturally
- "zarurat" includes consumer, work, service, factory, shop, taxi
- public understands "sahi mool" as right value/source

Concern:
- "mool" may be read as price only by some users, but that is still useful for commerce.

### 2. Zarurat Ko Mile Sahi Mool

Meaning:
- every need finds the right value/source

Why it works:
- warmer than option 1
- sounds more human and fulfilment-led

Concern:
- slightly longer.

### 3. Sahi Mool Se Har Kaam

Meaning:
- every task/action through the right source/value

Why it works:
- includes work, service, commerce and fulfilment
- less marketplace-like

Concern:
- "har kaam" can feel broad.

### 4. Roz Ki Zarurat, Sahi Mool Se

Meaning:
- daily needs through the right source/value

Why it works:
- household-friendly
- good for repeat app usage

Concern:
- may lean consumer/daily-needs more than manufacturer.

### 5. Sahi Mool, Sahi Kaam

Meaning:
- right value/source, right action/work

Why it works:
- short
- memorable
- works for product, service, work and payment

Concern:
- may sound like a campaign line, not a full tagline.

### 6. Mool Se Mile Har Zarurat

Meaning:
- every need fulfilled from the source

Why it works:
- source/directness comes through
- aligns with not being a marketplace/listing

Concern:
- grammar is slightly poetic.

### 7. Har Kaam Ka Sahi Mool

Meaning:
- every work/action has the right value/source

Why it works:
- good for workers, captains, services and commerce

Concern:
- consumer buying may feel slightly less visible.

### 8. Jahan Zarurat Ka Mool Mile

Meaning:
- where every need finds its source/value

Why it works:
- broad and emotionally understandable

Concern:
- "jahan" may make it sound like a place/marketplace.

### 9. Sahi Mool Tak Seedha

Meaning:
- direct to the right source/value

Why it works:
- direct operating app meaning
- strong against marketplace/listing perception

Concern:
- needs a subject; may feel incomplete alone.

### 10. Har Zarurat, Seedha Mool Tak

Meaning:
- every need, direct to source/value

Why it works:
- excellent for direct commerce/source/manufacturer
- public can understand the benefit

Concern:
- work/social layers less explicit but can still fit.

### 11. Sahi Mool Par Har Zarurat

Meaning:
- every need at the right value/source

Why it works:
- simple commerce trust

Concern:
- price-heavy.

### 12. Kaam, Zarurat Aur Bharosa

Meaning:
- work, need and trust

Why it works:
- includes users beyond buyer/seller
- avoids MLM language

Concern:
- does not use Mool meaning.

### 13. Sahi Zarurat. Sahi Mool.

Meaning:
- right need, right value/source

Why it works:
- clean and premium
- short enough for boards

Concern:
- abstract; not enough action.

### 14. Har Roz Ka Sahi Mool

Meaning:
- right value/source for every day

Why it works:
- memory-friendly
- daily habit positioning

Concern:
- price/daily-needs leaning.

### 15. Bharat Ka Sahi Mool

Meaning:
- India's right source/value

Why it works:
- big, national, premium

Concern:
- may sound too broad and legally/common-word risky as a tagline.

### 16. Mool Se Judta Har Kaam

Meaning:
- every action connects to source/value

Why it works:
- meaningful for direct source and work

Concern:
- "judta" could mildly return to network feeling, but softer than log-join language.

### 17. Zarurat Se Mool Tak

Meaning:
- from need to source/value

Why it works:
- very aligned with demand aggregation
- premium and concise

Concern:
- a bit strategic/abstract for lowest-common-denominator public.

### 18. Sahi Mool, Har Roz

Meaning:
- right value/source every day

Why it works:
- very short
- consumer and retailer friendly

Concern:
- not enough work/social purpose.

### 19. Zarurat Ka Seedha Mool

Meaning:
- direct source/value of need

Why it works:
- direct, trusted, source-first

Concern:
- can sound slightly unusual but memorable.

### 20. Har Zarurat Ka Mool Yahin

Meaning:
- the source/value of every need is here

Why it works:
- mass public line
- memorable and emotionally confident

Concern:
- "yahin" may feel marketplace/place-like.

## Best Shortlist

### Best Overall

> **Har Zarurat Ka Sahi Mool**

Why:
- broadest user relevance
- normal Indian public can understand
- not MLM
- not technical
- uses MoolSocial's name meaning
- works on grocery, taxi, factory, shop and public boards

### Best Demand-to-Source Line

> **Zarurat Se Mool Tak**

Why:
- strongest alignment with direct demand aggregation
- premium and strategic

### Best Public Board Line

> **Sahi Mool Se Har Kaam**

Why:
- short
- includes work/service/product/action
- feels useful across user types

### Best Consumer-Friendly Line

> **Roz Ki Zarurat, Sahi Mool Se**

Why:
- immediately understandable for households and shops

### Best Direct Source Line

> **Har Zarurat, Seedha Mool Tak**

Why:
- clear direct-source promise
- avoids marketplace grid perception

## Recommendation

Primary tagline:

> **MoolSocial - Har Zarurat Ka Sahi Mool**

Secondary tagline:

> **MoolSocial - Sahi Mool Se Har Kaam**

Strategic/internal line:

> **MoolSocial - Zarurat Se Mool Tak**
