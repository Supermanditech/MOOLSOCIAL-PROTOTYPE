# Brand Name Round 20 - India Platform Pattern Pairs

Date: 2026-06-25

## Why Round 19 was rejected

Round 19 sounded more professional than the earlier slogan-like names, but it still failed the deeper requirement:

- Not enough relation to India.
- Not enough relation to the operating-platform objective.
- Not enough cultural/emotional memory.
- Too much like neutral startup syllables.
- Not strong enough as a mouth brand for Indian consumers, drivers, grocers, restaurants, creators, manufacturers, workers, and families.

This round therefore uses pattern discovery.

Rule: one strategic naming pattern, two name candidates.

The goal is not to lock one name immediately. The goal is to find the right naming territory.

## Legal and screening caveat

These are creative candidates, not cleared trademarks.

Before locking any final name:

- Search exact wordmark on IP India.
- Search phonetic wordmark on IP India.
- Check relevant classes, especially 9, 35, 36, 38, 39, 41, 42.
- Run MCA company/LLP check.
- Check Google Play, App Store, domains, and social handles.
- Take trademark attorney opinion.

IP India public search supports wordmark and phonetic search, but has captcha and must be checked manually.

## Early Observations

The strongest strategic territories are:

1. Samyo / Samvay - integration, coordination, one super-app flow.
2. Setura / Setuva - bridge between consumer demand and verified fulfilment.
3. Sutraya / Sutrava - orchestrator of many life and work layers.
4. Chaupal / Chauko - Indian social operating square.
5. Artha / Arthya - money, value, business, and life meaning.

The weakest territories are:

1. Yantro / Chakro - may feel too mechanical or spiritual.
2. Karyo / Karyam - may feel regional or work-only.
3. Pramaan / Nirnay - may feel like a document/government/legal app.
4. Dhara / Pravah - emotionally good, but may be too soft for a super-app.

## Quick public-risk notes from visible searches

These are not final legal opinions:

- Samvay has visible Indian business usage.
- Sangra had visible company/public-person noise, so it was not retained as a main candidate.
- Setura has visible international business-consulting usage.
- Chaupal has visible trademark/app/media usage, so it is a strong cultural pattern but risky as a direct name.
- Addaro had multiple visible IndiaFilings records, so it was removed.
- Gatva has a visible Indian private limited company record, so it is high risk.
- Arthya has visible Indian tech/wealth company usage, so it is high risk.
- Prava was removed because visible trademark/company records exist.
- Jantra has visible public usage and needs caution.

## Best Next Step

Do not pick a final name from this table yet.

First choose the winning pattern:

- Integration pattern?
- Bridge pattern?
- Sutradhar/orchestrator pattern?
- Indian social square pattern?
- Artha/value pattern?

Once the pattern is selected, create 50 stricter variants only inside that pattern and screen heavily.

## My current instinct

The most strategically suitable direction is:

**Sutradhar / Samvay / Setu**

Reason: SuperMandi is not a marketplace. It is a direct operating system. The name should communicate coordination, bridging, and orchestration without making the app look like only ecommerce, taxi, grocery, or social media.
