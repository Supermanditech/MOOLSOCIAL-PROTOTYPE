# Brand Name Round 14 - Yara Direction Public Search

User direction:

Search next after checking Yara.

Goal:

Continue from Yara's good qualities:

- chaiwala easy
- secular
- friendly
- short
- emotional
- not category locked

But avoid public conflict/crowding.

## Search Result Summary

This is only quick public web screening, not final legal clearance.

### Rejected / High Risk

1. **Yara**
   - Yara India official presence exists.
   - Yara FarmCare and YaraConnect apps exist.
   - Visible trademark records across relevant and adjacent classes.
   - Reject.

2. **Vamo**
   - Visible VAMO trademark in India.
   - VAMO Systems / VAMOS vehicle tracking company visible in India.
   - Vamo delivery/driver/food apps visible internationally.
   - Reject for SuperMandi scope.

3. **Navo**
   - Navo B2B wholesale fashion app visible in India.
   - App claims registered/trademark rights in terms.
   - Visible class 42 and other records.
   - Reject; too close to commerce/retail/wholesale.

4. **Zava**
   - Visible India trademark records.
   - Visible pharma/healthcare use.
   - Zava online doctor/pharmacy brand visible internationally.
   - Reject.

5. **Savi**
   - Visible class 9 record.
   - Savi.ai finance app visible.
   - Savi Technology supply chain brand visible globally.
   - Savi India fashion/retail visible.
   - Reject.

6. **Kava**
   - Visible India trademark records in several classes.
   - Kava TV app visible.
   - Kava has product/category meaning globally.
   - Reject.

7. **Tavi**
   - Strong medical acronym association: Transcatheter Aortic Valve Implantation.
   - TAVI medical app visible.
   - Reject due to medical meaning and confusion.

8. **Sava**
   - SAVA Healthcare India is a major visible pharma company.
   - SAVA finance app visible internationally.
   - Visible trademark records.
   - Reject.

9. **Vano**
   - Visible India trademark records, including class 9 and class 11.
   - Reject.

10. **Zavo**
    - Visible India fintech/loan/EMI platform.
    - Visible class 35 record.
    - Reject; too close to money/EMI layer.

11. **Lavo**
    - Visible class 9 software mark for LAVO PARCEL.
    - Visible restaurant/hospitality usage.
    - Reject.

12. **Ravo**
    - Visible India trademark records.
    - Visible app use internationally.
    - Visible vehicle/equipment brand.
    - Reject.

### Lower But Still Not Clean

1. **Pavo**
   - Some visible records exist, but not as directly close as Navo/Zavo/Vamo.
   - Problem: sounds weaker and less Indian-mouth lovable.

2. **Mavo**
   - Sparse visible direct app conflict in quick search, but "mavo/mava" has tobacco/food associations in Indian contexts.
   - Not ideal.

3. **Vami**
   - Visible Vami Lifestyle, Vami Beverages and local food usage.
   - Not a direct app killer from quick scan, but still crowded enough to be cautious.

4. **Bavo**
   - Less visible India app/trademark collision in quick search.
   - Problem: Saint Bavo / European religious association; not ideal for all-religion mass India.

5. **Vabi**
   - Sparse visible conflict, but may sound like "bhabhi/vabi" in some Indian speech contexts.
   - Not good as a national consumer super-app.

## Conclusion

The Yara-like four-letter zone is dangerous because simple names are already crowded.

Do not lock:

- Yara
- Vamo
- Navo
- Zava
- Savi
- Kava
- Tavi
- Sava
- Vano
- Zavo
- Lavo
- Ravo

## Next Search Direction

Move slightly away from exact four-letter CVCV names and test:

- 5-letter names with two clear syllables
- still chaiwala-pronounceable
- still secular
- still carrying hidden A/V or Aarya/Vivaan letters
- less common than Yara/Vamo/Navo pattern

Examples to explore next:

- Vaani-like but not religious/common
- Yana-like but legally cleaner
- Vela/Velaa variants but not exact
- Avara-like but easier than Avora
- invented 5-letter names with simple mouth flow
