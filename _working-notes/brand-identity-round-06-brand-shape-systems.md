## New Parameter

This round avoids:
- standalone symbolic icon
- monogram-first approach
- leaf/person/infinity
- arrow/check/flow
- O-letter wordmark trick
- old source/trust/action icon logic

New direction:

> brand-shape systems that behave like compact official labels for app UI, QR stickers, print, boards and partner displays.

## Concepts Created

### Concept A - Split Brand Tag

Mool and Social are split into two joined blocks.

Strength:
- most practical for app headers and QR stickers
- visually separates source and social layer

Risk:
- can feel label-like if not refined with custom type.

### Concept B - Bold Plate

Hard-edged premium label with saffron shadow.

Strength:
- most premium print/board identity
- high recall in offline environments

Risk:
- uses MS in app icon variant; if no monogram is allowed, app icon must be redesigned.

### Concept C - Social Capsule

Mool carries Social inside one capsule.

Strength:
- communicates one integrated product

Risk:
- may feel like a badge/label rather than classic app logo.

### Concept D - India Signal Plate

MoolSocial on a restrained India-inspired side signal plate.

Strength:
- strong public board identity

Risk:
- must be careful not to drift toward flag-like colour blocks.

## Current Recommendation

Most practical:

> **Concept A - Split Brand Tag**

Most premium:

> **Concept B - Bold Plate**

Most product-integrated:

> **Concept C - Social Capsule**
