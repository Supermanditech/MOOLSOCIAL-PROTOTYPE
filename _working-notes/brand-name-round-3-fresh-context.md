# Brand Name Round 3 - Fresh Context

Status: working note only. Not final legal advice. Do not update final screenbook visuals until name is approved.

## Fresh Context

This round ignores the previous two naming frames:

- Not certainty/action-first.
- Not social-first only.

New frame:

Create a national daily operating network brand. It should feel like the digital version of India's everyday meeting point: where people talk, buy, book, ride, work, pay, prove, sell, source and repeat.

## Top Pick

`Chowko`

Reason:

Chowk is culturally understood as a public meeting point in India. Shops are near the chowk. Autos wait near the chowk. People hear local news at the chowk. Local commerce, services, social proof, workers, restaurants and customers all naturally belong there.

The spelling `Chowko` makes it more app-like and potentially more ownable than plain `Chowk`.

Possible lines:

- `Chowko - Pay. Buy. Book. Work. Talk.`
- `Chowko pe daal. Kaam chalu.`
- `India ka daily operating app.`
