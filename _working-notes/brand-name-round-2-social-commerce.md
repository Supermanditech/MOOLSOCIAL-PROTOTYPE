# Brand Name Round 2 - Social-Commerce Lens

Status: working note only. Not final legal advice. Do not update final screenbook visuals until name is approved.

## Changed Naming Lens

Round 1 focused on action, certainty and fulfilment.

Round 2 adds the missing social layer:

- posts
- reels
- creator proof
- local updates
- chat
- groups
- business pages
- community trust
- offer education
- social-to-commerce conversion

The name should not sound only like delivery, only work, only grocery, or only payments. It should feel like a place where people talk, discover, transact, book, earn and prove outcomes.

## Names Excluded After Quick Scan

- `Baatlo`: strong social fit, but a live BaatLo platform/site exists.
- `Jodo`: existing fintech/app presence.
- `Aayo`: app/social handle noise.
- `Rozzo`: app noise.
- `Nipto`: existing app name.
- `Hukum`: app-like usage/noise.
- `Saathi`: heavily used, including government/public app context.
- `Samvaad`: already used heavily, including AI/voice and public-sector contexts.

## Current Best Round 2 Pick

`Humzo`

Reason:

It is social-first without becoming only entertainment. `Hum` carries collective Indian emotion: us, people, family, locality, business community, work groups and creator audiences. The `zo` ending makes it app-like and distinctive.

Possible lines:

- `Humzo - Social se commerce tak.`
- `Humzo - Pay. Buy. Book. Work. Share.`
- `Humzo pe daal, kaam chalu.`

## Alternate Strong Pick

`Sanjho`

Reason:

It carries shared value and community demand. It fits group buying, social proof, shared savings, local trust and collective action.

Concern:

It is a common-language root, so it may be harder than a coined name unless the exact spelling is clear.
