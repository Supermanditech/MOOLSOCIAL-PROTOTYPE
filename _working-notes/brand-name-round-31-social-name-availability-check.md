# Brand Name Round 31 - Social Name Availability Check

Working note only. This is not legal clearance.

Checked names:
- SocialVend
- SangamOne
- SocialSetu
- ApnaSocial
- SocialCircle

## Important Limitation

Official India trademark clearance must be done on IP India Public Search with:
- exact wordmark search
- phonetic search
- class search, especially Classes 9, 35, 36, 38, 39, 41, 42 and possibly 45
- company/name/domain/app-store checks

IP India public search may require CAPTCHA/manual use:

https://tmrsearch.ipindia.gov.in/tmrpublicsearch/

This note is only a visible public-web conflict scan.

## 1. SocialVend

Meaning:
- social selling / social vendor commerce

Visible issue:
- Public web already shows SocialVend as an existing brand/product context outside India.
- The word also reads seller-first because "vend" means sell.
- Source examples:
  - https://www.socialvend.com/casestudies/mulberry-lights/
  - https://www.retailtouchpoints.com/news/socialvend-launches-socially-activated-vending-machines/17723/

Decision:
- **Reject for SuperMandi masterbrand.**

Reason:
- Too vendor/seller oriented.
- Not mass India enough.
- Not consumer-first.
- Visible public-use risk.

## 2. SangamOne

Meaning:
- one confluence/platform

Visible issue:
- "SangamOne" appears as an existing Indian technology/business brand context.
- Public references show SangamOne as connected to voice/video/data/Internet/mobile payment style services.
- Source examples:
  - https://www.instafinancials.com/Companies/S/CompanyList_S35.html
  - https://sode-edu.in/smvitm/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/DVV-3.5.1-5_MoUlist-18-19_compressed_compressed-1.pdf

Decision:
- **Reject / avoid.**

Reason:
- Too close to existing technology/service identity.
- Not worth spending legal energy unless user strongly loves it.

## 3. SocialSetu

Meaning:
- social bridge

Visible issue:
- Exact public trademark conflict was not conclusively confirmed in this quick web scan.
- However, both words are crowded: "Social" is weak/descriptive, and "Setu" has heavy India public/infrastructure usage.
- Likely weak protectability and high objection/confusion risk.
- Source examples:
  - https://www.instagram.com/socialsetu.co/
  - https://desigrowpanel.blogspot.com/
  - https://socialsetupanel.blogspot.com/

Decision:
- **Hold only as low-priority backup, not recommended.**

Reason:
- It may be available as an exact mark, but the name is not strong enough legally or emotionally for the masterbrand.
- It may feel government/platform infrastructure rather than premium mass consumer app.

## 4. ApnaSocial

Meaning:
- our social app

Visible issue:
- "Apna" is heavily used in India by major apps, brands and services.
- "Social" is descriptive and crowded.
- Combination may be difficult to protect strongly even if exact mark is not found.
- Source examples:
  - https://www.instagram.com/apna.social/
  - https://apps.apple.com/in/app/apna-konnect/id1673410045
  - https://www.invstt.com/company/apna-konnect
  - https://www.instagram.com/apnasocialbrand/

Decision:
- **Reject.**

Reason:
- Too common.
- Weak distinctiveness.
- Too social-media-only and not enough commerce/work/money.

## 5. SocialCircle

Meaning:
- social group/network

Visible issue:
- "Social Circle" appears in company/public usage contexts and is a very common social-network phrase.
- The term is highly descriptive for a social product.
- Source examples:
  - https://www.zaubacorp.com/SOCIAL-CIRCLE-INDIA-PRIVATE-LIMITED-U74999KA2021PTC147991
  - https://tracxn.com/d/legal-entities/india/social-circle-india-private-limited/___gHOHm63O_6Hw0YHBXDNr3D08XPlLXX0wuIfxUTjxqU
  - https://www.indiafilings.com/search/social-circle-india-private-limited-cin-U74999KA2021PTC147991

Decision:
- **Reject.**

Reason:
- Too common and descriptive.
- Too social-media-only.
- Weak protection potential.

## Overall Conclusion

These names confirm the problem with using **Social** directly:

- Social + common Indian/economic word becomes crowded.
- Social + business word becomes seller/vendor-first.
- Social + platform word becomes descriptive or infrastructure-like.
- The name starts looking like a social media or social selling app instead of a direct socio-commerce operating super-app.

## Recommendation

Stop using **Social** as a visible masterbrand prefix/suffix.

Better approach:

> Use social as a product principle and app layer, not the main brand word.

The masterbrand should imply:
- daily economic action
- people connection
- trust/proof
- buy/work/pay/chat
- India-scale usability

without literally using "Social" in the name.
