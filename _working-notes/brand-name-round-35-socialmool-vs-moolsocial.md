# Brand Name Round 35 - SocialMool vs MoolSocial

Working note only. Not final trademark clearance.

Date: 2026-06-25

## Current Primary Options

1. **SocialMool**
2. **MoolSocial**

Backup status:
- **MoolSocial** was earlier kept as an availability-looking backup.
- User now wants SocialMool and MoolSocial as the two primary options.

## Meaning

### SocialMool

Meaning impression:
- social + root/source/value
- social economy at the root
- people connection with value/source

Public perception:
- "Social" is understood quickly.
- "Mool" gives Indian value/root/source depth.

Risk:
- The first word "Social" may make users think it is a social media or marketing app.
- It sounds a little constructed: English word first, Indian root second.
- Trademark strength may be weaker because "Social" is descriptive/crowded.

### MoolSocial

Meaning impression:
- root/source/value-led social economy
- social network built from source/value/trust
- social commerce starting from the root, not from posts only

Public perception:
- More brand-like than SocialMool.
- "Mool" gives a stronger first brand identity.
- "Social" explains the people/community layer after the brand root.

Risk:
- Some users may first ask what "Mool" means.
- "Mool" itself has visible public use in India, including finance/fashion/social-development contexts.
- Official Class 9, 35, 36, 38, 39, 41, 42 and 45 searches are mandatory.

## Public Scan Signals

No strong exact public conflict was found for:
- SocialMool
- MoolSocial

## Comparison

| Factor | SocialMool | MoolSocial | Winner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Immediate category clue | Stronger | Medium | SocialMool |
| Premium masterbrand feel | Medium | Stronger | MoolSocial |
| Trademark distinctiveness | Medium-low due Social first | Medium, if compound clears | MoolSocial |
| Risk of looking like social media only | Higher | Lower | MoolSocial |
| Connection to source/value objective | Good | Stronger | MoolSocial |
| Street usage | "SocialMool pe dekh le" | "MoolSocial pe dekh le" | MoolSocial |
| Logo/app icon potential | SM, but close to SuperMandi initials | MS, cleaner new icon | MoolSocial |
| Consumer-first + business-ready | Medium | Stronger | MoolSocial |

## Recommendation

Choose **MoolSocial** as the primary candidate.

Keep **SocialMool** as the secondary candidate.

Reason:

> MoolSocial feels more like a real masterbrand. SocialMool explains faster, but it also sounds more descriptive and more like a social-media/agency product.

## Proposed Public Positioning

If the app name becomes **MoolSocial**, do not launch it alone.

Use this structure:

> MoolSocial - Buy, Work, Pay, Connect

or

> MoolSocial - India's Social Commerce & Work App

or

> MoolSocial - People, Products, Work and Money

Best short line:

> MoolSocial - Buy, Work, Pay, Connect

Why:
- avoids social-media-only perception
- explains the super-app purpose
- works on Play Store, app splash, first screen and investor deck
