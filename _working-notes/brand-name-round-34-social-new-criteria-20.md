# Brand Name Round 34 - Social Meaning, New Criteria 20

Working note only. Not legal clearance.

Date: 2026-06-25

## Backup Decision

**MoolSocial** is kept as the last backup plan.

Reason:
- It had fewer visible public conflicts than many earlier Social-prefix/suffix names.
- It connects to source/value/social economy.
- But it is not emotionally strong enough yet to be the first-choice masterbrand.

## New Criteria

This round avoids the earlier buckets:
- no direct Social prefix/suffix dependency
- no number pattern
- no Mandi/Arth/Jan/Lok crowding
- no pure invented empty syllables

New interpretation of social:

> social = people connection + trust + chat + proof + repeat relationship + commerce/work/payment action.

New roots:
- **Hum** = us / people-first / inclusive
- **Log** = people / public
- **Mila** = meet / found / connected
- **Jod** = connect / join
- **Sang** = together
- **Varta** = conversation / discussion
- **Mool** = source / root / value
- **Dwaar** = gateway
- **Setu** = bridge
- **Sutra** = thread/system

## Immediate Reject / Avoid From This Round

1. **LogMila**
   - Brazil transport company visible.

2. **HumMila**
   - Unrelated global/transliteration noise.
   - Not clean enough for first-choice masterbrand.

3. **LogMitra**
   - Searched during exploration, but dropped from final 20 because Mitra is too common and crowded.

## Better Names To Test Aloud

### Strongest New Direction

1. **MilaSetu**
   - Meaning: meeting bridge.
   - Why: social, commerce, work, money and services can all be "connected through the bridge."
   - Concern: Setu is common; official check needed.

2. **MilaJod**
   - Meaning: meet + connect.
   - Why: public meaning is easy and it is less formal than Setu names.
   - Concern: grammar is coined, not standard word.

3. **MilaMool**
   - Meaning: meet the source / find the root value.
   - Why: strongest for direct source + social trust + commerce.
   - Concern: Mool may not be understood equally by all users.

4. **HumJod**
   - Meaning: connect us.
   - Why: inclusive, mass India, consumer-first.
   - Concern: "Jod" may feel functional unless brand design is premium.

5. **JodMool**
   - Meaning: connect to source.
   - Why: closest to the platform's direct value-chain objective.
   - Concern: slightly more business/value-chain than consumer emotional.

6. **SamJod**
   - Meaning: society connected.
   - Why: short, meaningful, somewhat ownable.
   - Concern: may need pronunciation education.
