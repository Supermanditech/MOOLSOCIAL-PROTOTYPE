## Verdict Applied

The exact primary palette had strong visibility but was not premium, international, national-brandable or professionally polished.

The useful learning was retained:
- White on strong blue works well for night visibility.
- Blue can be the dominant public memory colour.
- Red and green must be controlled, not equal brand colours.

## Refined Palette

- Brand Blue: `#143D8F`
- Night Blue: `#071B45`
- Deep Blue: `#0B2E73`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Soft White: `#F6F8FC`
- Action Red: `#C92235`
- Trust Green: `#168346`

## Rules

1. Blue and white dominate the brand.
2. Red is only a small action/accent colour.
3. Green is only a trust/success cue.
4. Do not use red and green equally with blue in the main logo.
5. Night signboard should use white wordmark on deep blue.
