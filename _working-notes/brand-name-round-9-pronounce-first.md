# Brand Name Round 9 - Pronounce First

User direction:

The previous round is not easy enough to pronounce.

Parked fallback names:

- Yonda
- Omera

This round avoids the earlier criteria and optimizes for one thing first:

If an Indian consumer, auto captain, grocer, restaurant owner, manufacturer, creator or worker sees the name once, they should be able to say it correctly without asking how to pronounce it.

## New Parameters

Pronounce-first rules:

- two clear syllables
- no Q, X or difficult consonant clusters
- no spelling tricks
- no silent letters
- no English-only sound dependency
- no category-specific meaning
- should work in Hindi, English and most Indian-language mouth patterns
- should be easy on phone calls: "app ka naam ___ hai"

Phrase test:

- ___ pe daal
- ___ kar de
- ___ se kar
- ___ Pay
- ___ Work
- ___ Business
- ___ Captain
- ___ Creator
