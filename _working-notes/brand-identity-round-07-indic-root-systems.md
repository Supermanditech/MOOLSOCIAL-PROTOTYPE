## User Feedback Before This Round

The brand-shape systems were rejected.

## New Direction

This round uses subtle India-native identity through the root word **Mool**.

Avoided:
- standalone generic symbol
- monogram-first direction
- leaf/person/infinity
- arrow/check/flow
- wordmark O-letter trick
- label plates
- marketplace/cart/rupee symbols

New direction:

> Indic root identity: Latin brand remains MoolSocial, while a small Devanagari root mark expresses "Mool" as source/root.

## Concepts Created

### Concept A - Mool Root Mark

Uses "मू" as app icon and brand mark.

Strength:
- highly India-native
- distinctive app icon

Risk:
- may feel Hindi-script specific.

### Concept B - Small Mool Tag

Uses small "मूल" tag before the Latin MoolSocial wordmark.

Strength:
- best balance between national/international polish and Indian meaning
- Latin brand remains primary

Risk:
- still depends on Devanagari support and cultural acceptance.

### Concept C - Matra Signal

Uses script-inspired bars without readable Devanagari letters.

Strength:
- safest if the brand should feel Indian but not language-specific

Risk:
- less memorable as an app icon.

### Concept D - Root Stamp

Uses circular "मू" stamp mark.

Strength:
- good for public boards, QR stickers and trust mark feeling

Risk:
- stamp can feel traditional instead of consumer-tech.

## Current Recommendation

Best balanced direction:

> **Concept B - Small Mool Tag**

Safest non-language-specific direction:

> **Concept C - Matra Signal**
