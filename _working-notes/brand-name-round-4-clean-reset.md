# Brand Name Round 4 - Clean Reset

Context reset from the first three rounds.

This round avoids forced Hindi-sounding endings such as `-o`, `-zo`, `-lo`.
The naming direction is: short, national-mouth-friendly, consumer-first, trust-led, social-ready, commerce/work/money capable, and capable of becoming a phrase like "___ kar de" or "___ pe daal".

## Naming Principle

SuperMandi's final consumer app name should not sound like only:

- a marketplace
- a delivery app
- a jobs app
- a taxi app
- a fintech app
- a social media app

It must sound like a daily operating brand where people can:

- ask
- post
- buy
- book
- pay
- work
- earn
- chat
- get things done

## Strongest Direction In This Round

The most promising emotional/strategic direction is a name around:

- correctness
- trust
- fair price
- right action
- daily confidence
- social proof
- assured outcome

That points toward names like `Sahi`, but exact legal clearance may be difficult because it is a common word.

## 20 New Names

1. **Sahi**
   - Meaning signal: right, correct, fair, trusted.
   - Why it fits: decision-ready cards, fair price, proof, money, work, daily trust.
   - Mouth phrase: "Sahi pe dekh", "Sahi se order kar", "Sahi kar de."
   - Risk: common word; likely harder trademark path.

## My Current Honest Pick

**Sahi** is the closest to the heart of the business model.

Reason:
SuperMandi is not about showing many options. It is about helping users reach the right next action with trust, price, proof, payment, refund/cancellation and fulfilment clarity.

That is exactly the emotional promise of `Sahi`.

But because `Sahi` is common, it needs serious trademark screening before we emotionally lock it.
