# Brand Name Round 7 - Emotional Masterbrand

User direction:

Yonda is liked and can remain as a last preference, but it feels emotionally empty. Create a completely different round of names that preserve brandability but add emotional pull.

This round does not use previous filters as the main driver.

Core emotional requirement:

The app name should feel like people are entering a trusted daily life network, not just opening a utility app.

The name should be able to hold:

- consumer confidence
- social belonging
- creator identity
- business growth
- driver/captain dignity
- manufacturer opportunity
- money trust
- work/earning
- local life

## Strongest Current Recommendation

**Arova**

Reason:

It feels emotionally fuller than Yonda but still broad enough to be a super-app master brand. It carries uplift and progress without becoming only social, grocery, work, ride, payment or manufacturing.

Possible brand architecture:

- Arova
- Arova Pay
- Arova Work
- Arova Business
- Arova Captain
- Arova Creator
- Arova Chat
- Arova Social

## Yonda Position

Yonda remains parked as a clean, neutral fallback. It has good phonetic simplicity, but this round aims to find a name that has emotion from day one.
