## New Parameter

This round removes old symbolic logic:
- no person
- no leaf
- no infinity
- no shield
- no arrow
- no flow symbol
- no source/trust/action icon logic
- no standalone abstract operating mark

New direction:

> Build memory from the wordmark itself.

## Concepts Created

### Concept A - Double-O Device

The two O letters in Mool become the brand memory device and app icon.

Strength:
- most memorable
- most compact for favicon/app icon
- creates a repeatable brand element

Risk:
- can look unusual in text if not refined carefully.

### Concept B - Word + Saffron Signal

Clean text-led wordmark with one small saffron signal dot.

Strength:
- most international and minimal
- works well for print, website nav, stickers and boards

Risk:
- app icon crop is less distinctive because it depends on the word.

### Concept C - Commerce Pill

Rounded brand capsule with saffron vertical signal.

Strength:
- strongest for offline stickers, partner boards and QR materials

Risk:
- can feel more like a label than a pure app logo.

### Concept D - Stacked Word Signal

Mool and Social separated in a stacked word layout.

Strength:
- good for campaign posters and large public boards

Risk:
- not compact enough for app headers.

## Current Recommendation

Best for masterbrand:

> **Concept B - Word + Saffron Signal**

Best for app icon/favicons:

> **Concept A - Double-O Device**

Best for offline partner material:

> **Concept C - Commerce Pill**
