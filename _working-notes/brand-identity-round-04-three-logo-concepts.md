## User Feedback Before This Round

The previous MoolSocial icon was rejected because it was not good enough.

New requirement:
- prepare 3 completely different versions
- no person / leaf / infinity / monogram direction
- compact for app header, QR sticker, print, boards and website navigation

## Concepts Created

### Concept A - Origin Arrow

Meaning:
- need/source point moving into action

Strength:
- most action-oriented
- compact and clear in app header and QR sticker

Risk:
- arrow can feel generic if not refined further

### Concept B - Verified Core

Meaning:
- verified source, trust and proof before commerce/work/payment action

Strength:
- most serious and trustworthy
- best for factory, retailer, payment/proof and national brand feeling

Risk:
- check/hexagon can become generic if not customized further

### Concept C - Mool Flow

Meaning:
- layered daily actions moving through one operating app

Strength:
- most app/platform-like
- good for super-app operating layer meaning

Risk:
- may feel like menu/stack icon and less emotionally memorable

## Current Recommendation

Best direction to refine:

> **Concept B - Verified Core**

Reason:
- strongest professional trust signal
- no MLM/social-media/marketplace/leaf/person confusion
- works best across manufacturer, retailer, worker, payment and consumer contexts

Second-best:

> **Concept A - Origin Arrow**

Reason:
- clearer action signal
- simpler for QR/app header
