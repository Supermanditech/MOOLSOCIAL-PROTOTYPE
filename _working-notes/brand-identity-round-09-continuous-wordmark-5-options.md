## User Constraints

User rejected:
- M-only icon
- MS icon
- Mool and Social shown as separate blocks
- weak icon meaning
- old symbolic parameters

User asked for:
- custom wordmark-first logo
- full **MoolSocial** stays together
- compact
- professional perceived meaning
- national and international brand feeling
- visible day and night
- strong for printing, website, app, QR stickers and offline boards

## New Criteria Applied

- No standalone M icon
- No MS icon
- No split Mool/Social blocks
- No separate symbol-first logo
- Full **MoolSocial** remains the identity
- App icon uses compact full-name crop, not letter initials
- Deep teal, night ink and saffron accent chosen for day/night/print visibility

## Options Created

### Option 1 - Continuous Teal Signal

Full MoolSocial wordmark in deep teal with saffron accent only on the natural i-dot.

Best for:
- primary app logo
- website nav
- app header
- QR stickers
- general production use

### Option 2 - Nightline Wordmark

Near-black wordmark with teal/saffron baseline.

Best for:
- print
- invoices
- documentation
- daytime readability

### Option 3 - Graphite + Teal Wordmark

MoolSocial continuous wordmark with Mool in teal and Social in graphite.

Best for:
- manufacturer/factory/business trust
- serious public boards

### Option 4 - Compact Rounded Wordmark

Full MoolSocial in one rounded outline capsule.

Best for:
- QR stickers
- shop stickers
- taxi stickers
- bounded app header surfaces

### Option 5 - Night Brand Wordmark

Night-first high-contrast MoolSocial wordmark.

Best for:
- outdoor/night visibility
- taxi boards
- launch posters
- dark UI
