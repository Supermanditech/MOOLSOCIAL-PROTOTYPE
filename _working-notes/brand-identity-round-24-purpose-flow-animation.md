## Reason For This Round

The earlier animated `oo` concept could be read as spectacles or lenses, which may create an unwanted association with eyewear brands. The new direction keeps the `oo` letters static and moves the animation away from the letters.

## New Animation Direction

The motion is now a purpose-flow signal under the wordmark:

1. Need enters through the app.
2. MoolSocial matches the need with a verified option.
3. The user moves into action: commerce, chat, work, money, service or fulfilment.
4. Trust closes the loop through proof, payment, refund/cancellation or next step.

This animation is meant to say what MoolSocial does instead of decorating the letters.
