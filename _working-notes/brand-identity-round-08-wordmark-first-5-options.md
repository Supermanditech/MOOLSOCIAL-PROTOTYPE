## User Preference

User selected this logo type:

> Use a custom wordmark-first logo, not icon-first. The main identity should be MoolSocial itself.

## Criteria Used

- Main identity is the written word **MoolSocial**
- No standalone icon-first direction
- No symbolic meaning forced into the mark
- Compact horizontal usage first
- App icon is a crop/derivative of wordmark style
- Must work in app header, QR sticker, invoice, website nav, shop board, taxi sticker and factory board
- Deep teal primary colour
- Small saffron accent only
- No Indian flag, tricolour layout, rupee, cart, storefront, leaf, person, check, arrow, infinity, shield or MLM/network visuals

## Options Created

### Option 1 - Clean Signal Wordmark

Clean text-led mark with small saffron signal between Mool and Social.

Recommended as primary app logo because:
- compact
- readable
- polished
- easiest to use across app/web/QR/print

### Option 2 - Split Wordmark

Mool and Social in connected blocks.

Recommended for:
- offline partner boards
- shop/taxi/factory stickers
- QR and invoices

### Option 3 - Underline Motion Wordmark

Single word with a subtle underline motion bar.

Recommended for:
- consumer app identity where readability matters most

### Option 4 - Premium Plate Wordmark

Wordmark inside a bold plate with saffron shadow.

Recommended for:
- outdoor/public boards
- launch posters
- packaging/print

### Option 5 - Stacked Campaign Wordmark

Mool and Social stacked.

Recommended only for:
- campaign layouts
- posters
- larger public boards

Not recommended for app header because it is too tall.

## Current Recommendation
