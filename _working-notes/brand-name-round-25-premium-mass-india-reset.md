# Brand Name Round 25 - Premium Mass India Reset

Date: 2026-06-25

## Why Round 24 Was Rejected

Round 24 correctly identified that the name must be easy for Indians to speak.

But the direction became too low-market and casual.

Rejected framing:

- "Bhai, ___ pe dekh le."
- "___ kar de."
- "___ se payment bhej."
- "___ pe kaam mil gaya."
- "Mummy bhi ___ use kar legi."
- "Captain ko ___ se ride mili."

Reason:

- These tests make the brand feel cheap.
- They pull the naming direction toward local slang.
- They reduce a national operating super-app into a casual street utility.
- They do not match the seriousness of a platform handling money, work, business, fulfilment, proof, social identity, and commerce.

## New Correct Position

The name must be:

**Premium mass India.**

Meaning:

- Easy for every Indian to pronounce.
- Not slangy.
- Not local-market cheap.
- Not overly corporate.
- Not foreign and empty.
- Not Sanskrit-heavy or government-like.
- Professional enough for manufacturers, investors, institutions, restaurants, creators, retailers and financial partners.
- Warm enough for households, auto captains, workers, shopkeepers and daily users.

## Correct Speech Test

Use dignified mass-market sentences instead:

- "Open ___."
- "Check it on ___."
- "Pay through ___."
- "My shop is on ___."
- "The order came on ___."
- "Book it through ___."
- "I received work on ___."
- "The customer confirmed on ___."
- "Our business uses ___."
- "The family uses ___ for daily needs."

These are simple, but not cheap.

## Brand Positioning Standard

The name should be able to stand beside:

- Jio
- Tata Neu
- PhonePe
- Aadhaar
- UPI
- Swiggy
- Zomato
- Blinkit
- Rapido
- Meesho

But it should not imitate any of them.

## What To Avoid Now

Avoid:

- Casual slang.
- Joke-like names.
- Verb-command names.
- Names that sound like local errand apps.
- Names that sound like small delivery apps.
- Names that sound like a WhatsApp group or neighborhood helper.
- Names that sound like a scheme, NGO, religious initiative, or government portal.
- Overly technical abbreviation names.
- Forced startup syllables.

## What To Seek Now

Seek:

- Country-scale simplicity.
- Indian warmth.
- Institutional trust.
- Consumer ease.
- Business seriousness.
- Strong app-icon identity.
- A name that can carry social, commerce, work, money, chat, proof and fulfilment.

## Naming Source Should Come From

The name can draw from hidden ideas like:

- India-scale demand
- value movement
- direct fulfilment
- trust and proof
- household and business together
- economic operating network
- daily life utility
- verified work
- social commerce
- demand-to-outcome

But the final word should feel like a brand, not an explanation.

## New Naming Filter

A candidate must pass all five:

1. Would a manufacturer take this name seriously?
2. Would a household trust this name?
3. Would a creator be proud to show this name on profile and links?
4. Would an auto captain or worker pronounce it easily without feeling it is childish?
5. Would investors believe this could become a national platform?

If any answer is no, reject it.

## Next Round Requirement

- Premium mass India.
- Not slang.
- Not too common.
- Not too foreign.
