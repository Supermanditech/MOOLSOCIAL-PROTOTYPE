## 3. Customer And Retention Rules

Customer history is purpose-limited. It may show orders, invoices, repeat baskets, last purchase, delivery preference, open issue, eligible loyalty benefit, customer credit where enabled and communication permission.

Invoice delivery permission is separate from marketing permission. Marketing, reminders, back-in-stock messages, group-price invitations and offers use only permitted channels. Refusing marketing never blocks an invoice or receipt.

## 4. Offers And Campaign Rules

Retailer offers are direct executable commerce actions, not promotional listings. Every campaign defines:

```text
objective
eligible products or basket
audience and geography
start and end
price or benefit
stock/capacity commitment
budget and maximum payable amount
attribution rule
cancellation and refund effect
approval and audit history
```

Creator, freelancer or field-team execution remains MoolSocial-operated and verified. The retailer does not browse an unmanaged worker directory.

## 5. Slow Stock Liquidation Rules

Liquidation uses stock-ledger truth and protects retailer margin, product safety, expiry/sell-by constraints, supplier restrictions and consumer trust.

Controlled outcomes may include:

```text
customer clearance offer
bundle with faster-moving products
nearby retailer transfer or auction
return-to-supplier claim where eligible
donation or compliant disposal where applicable
```

Publishing requires selected quantity, floor price, reason, channel, time window and approval. Reserved or disputed quantity cannot be liquidated.

## 6. AI Operating Assistant Rules

AI may explain, forecast and draft actions from authorized workspace data. It may propose restocking, offer creation, stock checks, customer follow-up, invoice retrieval, expense classification and reconciliation.

AI cannot autonomously:

```text
spend money
change prices
publish campaigns
place procurement orders
refund customers
message customers
change staff permissions
file statutory returns
```

Every consequential action requires explicit operator approval and an audit record showing source data, recommendation, approval and result.

## 7. Staff And Permission Rules

Permissions are workspace and branch scoped. Initial controlled roles:

```text
Owner
Manager
Counter Operator
Stock Operator
Procurement Operator
Delivery Coordinator
Accountant
Marketing Operator
View Only
```

Owner-only or specifically delegated actions include bank/payment mandate changes, refunds above limit, staff administration, plan cancellation, statutory filing approval, period lock and sensitive exports.

## 8. Store Settings And Readiness

Store settings own branch profile, hours, order acceptance, catalog publication, pickup/delivery modes, service area, payment, invoice/GST settings, refund rule, support contact, licences/reminders and consumer visibility readiness.

Settings must surface an actionable readiness result. Registration alone never publishes products or services.

## 9. Returns And Customer Issues

Customer issues retain order, item, payment, fulfilment, evidence, communication, SLA and resolution state. The retailer may accept, request evidence, propose replacement, refund under authorization or escalate. Customer statutory and platform rights are not reduced by shop policy.

Supplier-side claims remain linked to procurement/goods-receipt screens and are not mixed with customer returns.

## 10. Cash, Bank, Expenses And Reconciliation

The money-control screen composes authoritative sales, payment, purchase and settlement records. It supports cash, UPI, card and bank position, expense capture, transfer records, mismatch resolution and period/accountant controls.

No transaction already completed through MoolSocial requires duplicate entry. Manual records must carry operator, source evidence and audit history.
