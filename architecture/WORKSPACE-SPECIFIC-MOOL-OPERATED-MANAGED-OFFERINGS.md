## 1. Refined Decision

MoolSocial will offer concrete managed business packages, not only recommendations such as “grow sales” or “improve delivery.”

The package must say:

```text
what the user asks MoolSocial to take responsibility for
what MoolSocial will operate
what the user must provide
what exact result is delivered
who owns inventory, customer, invoice and refund responsibility
how the user pays
what is excluded
what happens when the result cannot be completed
```

Examples:

- **Reserve riders for my shop**, not “Delivery Support.”
- **Source these products at or below my target landed price**, not “Procurement Solutions.”
- **Assign a sales team to sell household baskets**, not “Grow Sales.”
- **Sell eligible slow stock to MoolSocial at its accepted buying quote**, not “Liquidation Assistance.”
- **Complete my monthly GST and books package**, not “Compliance Services.”

## 2. Five Commercial Models

These models must remain distinct in contracts, UI, invoices and backend logic.

### A. Reserved Capacity Service

MoolSocial reserves an eligible rider, worker, vehicle, operator or specialist pool for defined shifts, geography and capacity.

Commercial form:

```text
monthly or shift reservation fee
+ per completed delivery/job/trip
+ disclosed tax or third-party cost
```

“Dedicated” means reserved service capacity with primary and backup assignment. It does not imply that a person is owned by the business or that unlimited work can be imposed.

### B. Managed Sourcing Mandate

The business submits required products/inputs, specification, quantity, target landed price, delivery place and required date. MoolSocial sources, compares, negotiates and presents a decision-ready offer.

Commercial form:

```text
monthly sourcing desk
+ accepted purchase-order fee or disclosed procurement margin
+ logistics/tax
```

An **Unlimited Sourcing Desk** permits unlimited sourcing requests during the plan period. It never means unlimited quantity, credit, fulfilment or loss-bearing. Every purchase order remains separately accepted and funded.

### C. Results Workforce

MoolSocial assigns eligible field sales, marketing, creator, onboarding, customer-success or operating participants. The business does not browse unmanaged workers.

Commercial form:

```text
setup/operating fee
+ fee per retained paid sale, subscription, booking, contract or accepted activation
subject to one disclosed cap
```

Raw leads, visits, views and unfulfilled orders do not qualify result fees.

### D. MoolSocial Principal Purchase Or Capacity Reservation

MoolSocial may make a time-limited bid to buy eligible inventory, content rights, output or contracted capacity. This is not an entitlement and is not the same as marketplace liquidation.

Where goods are purchased:

```text
MoolSocial accepts product, batch, quantity, condition and buying rate
-> seller accepts the bid
-> seller issues the required invoice/document
-> MoolSocial or its authorized operator completes pickup and goods receipt
-> title and risk transfer under the accepted contract
-> seller is paid
-> MoolSocial owns later resale price, inventory loss and consumer obligation
```

The contract must identify:

- principal buyer and later seller of record;
- title/risk transfer point;
- GST invoice and input-credit treatment;
- food, storage, distribution and transport authority where applicable;
- batch, expiry, condition and recall responsibility;
- pickup, rejection, shortage and payment rules;
- consumer warranty, return and refund responsibility;
- maximum capital and stock exposure.

MoolSocial can also reserve future service or production capacity instead of buying perishable finished output. For example, it may fund 500 future tiffin meals or 100 off-peak salon appointments, then allocate paid consumer demand under disclosed terms.

### E. Managed Back Office And Compliance

MoolSocial coordinates verified professionals and software operations for books, reconciliation, returns, GST, ITR, licence readiness, payroll or profile-specific records.

Commercial form:

```text
fixed monthly package
+ separately approved professional/statutory fees
```

Completion is an accepted bookkeeping, reconciliation, submission or filing milestone. Tax savings, regulatory acceptance or professional outcomes are not guaranteed.

## 3. Retailer Package — Corrected Concrete Version

### 1. Shop Rider Reserve

User action:

```text
Choose shifts
-> choose service radius
-> enter expected orders and parcel limits
-> review reserved capacity, backup SLA and price
-> fund and activate
```

MoolSocial delivers:

- primary and backup eligible rider capacity;
- pickup and delivery orchestration;
- OTP/proof, cash reconciliation and failed-delivery handling;
- completed-delivery and SLA report.

Charge: capacity reservation plus per completed delivery.

### 2. Unlimited Target-Price Procurement Desk

User action:

```text
Add product/brand/pack/quantity
-> enter target landed price and required date
-> MoolSocial sources and compares
-> retailer accepts an exact offer
-> PO, delivery and GRN complete
```

MoolSocial delivers unlimited sourcing requests under the subscription, but each order has separate quantity, supplier, price, tax, delivery, payment and claim terms.

Charge: monthly desk plus accepted PO fee or disclosed margin.

### 3. Household Basket Sales Team

The retailer chooses eligible products, stock, acceptable selling/settlement price, service area and daily basket capacity. MoolSocial:

1. builds decision-ready household basket plans;
2. assigns funded sales/marketing participants;
3. sells monthly/weekly basket subscriptions to households;
4. allocates each order to an eligible retailer with fresh stock and capacity;
5. operates delivery, attribution, refund and participant commission;
6. pays the retailer according to the locked settlement terms.

Only paid, delivered and retained household basket cycles qualify a sales result.

### 4. MoolSocial Stock Buy

The retailer selects stock lines for sale to MoolSocial. The platform checks:

```text
eligible category and brand
source invoice and title
batch, condition and remaining shelf life
quantity and location
recall/restriction status
current MoolSocial buying rate and capital cap
```

MoolSocial may:

- make a principal purchase bid;
- offer a lower/higher quantity;
- reject the stock with a reason;
- instead offer managed marketplace liquidation.

After accepted pickup and goods receipt, the retailer receives the contracted purchase price. MoolSocial owns later resale risk. The UI must never imply automatic platform purchase before a bid exists.

### 5. Retail Business Back Office

The retailer chooses:

```text
Books only
Books + GST data and filing coordination
Books + GST + ITR coordination
Full monthly package with stock/order/payment reconciliation
```

MoolSocial shows required documents, assigned professional/operator, monthly milestones, missing data, completion acknowledgement and total fee.

## 4. Household Basket Operating Model

Launch should use the lower-capital allocation model:

```text
Household chooses basket and schedule
-> platform shows exact plan, seller/allocation rule and substitutions
-> subscription payment succeeds
-> allocation engine selects an eligible retailer
-> retailer accepts fresh stock/capacity
-> order terms lock
-> retailer packs
-> reserved delivery capacity fulfils
-> refund window passes
-> retailer settlement and sales-team earning qualify
```

Allocation considers:

```text
serviceability
fresh authoritative stock
locked retailer settlement price
fulfilment reliability
delivery capacity
consumer preferences
conflict and concentration limits
```

The consumer must approve material product/brand/quantity/price substitutions. A retailer timeout or rejection causes governed reallocation before consumer commitment or requires fresh disclosure when terms change.

Later, MoolSocial may offer a principal basket model using inventory it already owns. Marketplace and principal orders must never share ambiguous invoice, seller or refund responsibility.

## 8. Regulatory And Operating Gates

- CBIC's e-commerce FAQ distinguishes supplies by third-party sellers from goods an operator purchases and resells on its own account. Principal purchase creates two supplies and the operator's own-sale GST treatment, rather than being only marketplace TCS: https://cbic-gst.gov.in/hindi/sectoral-faq.html
- The Consumer Protection framework publishes the E-Commerce Rules and related consumer requirements. Seller identity, price, returns, grievance and responsibility must follow the selected marketplace or inventory model: https://consumeraffairs.nic.in/acts-and-rules/consumer-protection/consumer-protection
- FSSAI treats manufacturing, storage, transport, wholesale, distribution and retail as food-business activities with applicable registration/licence criteria. MoolSocial cannot buy, store and redistribute food stock as principal without the applicable operating setup: https://foscos.fssai.gov.in/search-eligibility/CL
- Dedicated riders, field workers and platform participants require current labour, worker-classification, social-security, safety, insurance and state-specific review. The Ministry of Labour & Employment recognizes gig/platform-worker policy and registration measures; the production model must be revalidated at launch: https://www.labour.gov.in/static/uploads/2025/06/f99af030ba41fe5578acc3fde78da4b6.pdf

This is a product and architecture contract, not legal or tax advice.

## 10. Final Locks

1. Every offering names the responsibility MoolSocial actually takes.
2. “Unlimited” applies only to requests unless quantity/value capacity is explicitly funded.
3. “Dedicated” means reserved, eligible capacity plus backup/SLA; worker rights and limits remain protected.
4. Results workforce is paid on retained transactions or accepted milestones, not leads/views.
5. MoolSocial principal purchase begins only after an accepted bid and goods receipt/title transfer.
6. Marketplace and principal inventory, invoice, tax, seller and refund responsibilities never mix.
7. Regulated profiles receive only permitted profile-specific offerings.
8. Every offering ends with a visible accepted result, exact evidence, total cost and recovery path.
