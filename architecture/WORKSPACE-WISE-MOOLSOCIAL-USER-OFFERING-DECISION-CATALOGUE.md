## 1. Proposed Market Position

MoolSocial should not lead with ordinary software, advertising impressions, followers, leads or generic business services.

The proposed category is:

> **MoolSocial converts future human and business needs into programmable demand, lets users earn toward what they want, and lets every user build a verified economic node from successful outcomes.**

The two-sided promise is:

### For an individual

```text
Tell Mool what you genuinely intend to buy or complete
-> receive competing eligible outcomes
-> send a verifiable signal
-> earn from genuine completed commerce or work
-> apply value toward the intended purchase
-> build greater trusted economic reach
```

### For a business or provider

```text
State the exact demand, sales, capacity, sourcing or operating result required
-> expose a funded offer to matched future needs
-> contract verified execution
-> pay for accepted work or controlled outcomes
-> convert completed outcomes into reusable demand and operating capacity
```

Ordinary software remains important, but it is supporting infrastructure:

- CoreOS records and operates the business.
- Mool Agent automates permitted work.
- Mool Outcome Network produces funded, verified activity.
- Mool Node accumulates the user's long-term economic capability.

## 2. Shared Product Language

These names are proposals for founder approval.

| Product | What the user receives | What it is not |
|---|---|---|
| **Mool Purchase Power** | A time-bound capacity to register planned purchases up to a displayed amount, proposed default INR 1,00,000 for 30 days | Cash, loan, withdrawable balance or promise that MoolSocial will pay INR 1,00,000 |
| **Mool Wish** | A product, service or life-task goal with product conditions, budget, geography and intended completion date | A casual like or non-actionable wishlist |
| **Future-Need Slot** | A purpose-consented future requirement that eligible providers may compete to fulfil | Personal data sold to advertisers |
| **Mool Goal Credit** | Clearly separated reserved, earned, settled and applied value funded by a business offer or verified work | An unfunded virtual number represented as cash |
| **Mool Signal** | A signed, attributable and expiring need/recommendation message with outcome lineage | Cryptocurrency, tradeable token or public personal-data blockchain |
| **Mool Pulse** | Controlled expansion of a useful Signal to progressively larger matched, opted-in audiences | Automatic bulk messaging or unsolicited contact spam |
| **Mool Reach Power** | Earned distribution capacity based on relevance, completed outcomes, satisfaction and low returns/complaints | A purchased follower count or unrestricted right to message anyone |
| **Funded Sales Signal** | A business-funded unit target, buyer benefit, reward pool, stock/capacity commitment and settlement rule | Unfunded “promote my product” request |
| **Benefit Imprint** | A business funds a measurable user benefit and receives disclosed attribution and permitted recall measurement | Hidden advertisement |
| **Outcome Contract** | Exact target, readiness, evidence, cap, price, make-good and completion rule | Vague revenue promise |
| **Mool Node** | The user's operating identity: verified outcomes, Reach Power, active capacity, agent, recipes, agreements and earnings history | Ownership of customers or their personal data |
| **Outcome Recipe** | A proven, permissioned method or automated playbook for producing a defined result | Guaranteed income or unreviewed autonomous action |
| **Managed Mool Service** | MoolSocial arranges accountable people, partners or operating capacity against a defined SLA | A directory or raw lead |
| **CoreOS** | Profile-specific software for orders, bookings, tasks, stock, money, proof and support | MoolSocial's primary market-dominating promise |

## 3. Permanent Personal Layer — Every User Receives This

Every account remains an Individual / Consumer / Social user even when it operates several workspaces.

### Proposed individual offering

1. **INR 1,00,000 Mool Purchase Power**
   - Proposed default duration: 30 days.
   - The amount is planning capacity, not money.
   - Confirming an INR 80,000 Mool Wish reserves INR 80,000 of the capacity.
   - Purchase, cancellation or expiry releases the capacity.
   - Genuine completion may increase the user's later capacity.

2. **Mool Wish And Future-Need Slots**
   - Product purchase.
   - Grocery or meal requirement.
   - Booking or service requirement.
   - Travel or recurring commute.
   - Work, earning or task goal.
   - Business purchase where the same person operates a workspace.

3. **Four Intent Confidence Levels**
   - Interested: saved only; not counted as demand.
   - Planned: budget and date confirmed; forecast only.
   - Reserved: stronger proof such as a permitted refundable reservation or regulated payment mandate.
   - Approved: authorizes purchase when stated conditions are met.

4. **Mool Goal Credit**
   - Reserved sponsor benefit.
   - Verified task or recommendation earning.
   - Group-price saving.
   - Settled amount.
   - Amount applied to the Mool Wish.
   - Returned/cancelled transactions reverse only the relevant conditional amount.

5. **Earn-Toward-My-Goal**
   - A user may complete eligible Mool work or produce a verified direct commerce outcome.
   - Genuine earned compensation remains separate from a purchase-only booster.
   - A business may add a transparent booster when settled earnings are applied to the declared goal.

6. **Mool Signal And Signal Lineage**
   - The user may send a signed Signal to selected contacts or matched Mool users.
   - Every recipient controls whether to ignore, confirm, buy or create their own branch.
   - The ledger attributes origin, direct contribution, purchase, return and dispute.

7. **Mool Pulse And Reach Power**
   - Initial reach is small and relevance-controlled.
   - Useful, converting, low-complaint Signals unlock progressively greater matched distribution.
   - Proposed tiers: Circle, Local, City, Region and India.
   - Reach can decrease after spam, deception, high returns, complaints or irrelevant distribution.

8. **Personal Mool Node**
   - Signal portfolio.
   - Intent Reliability.
   - Reach Power.
   - Goal Capital.
   - Verified outcome history.
   - Permitted Outcome Recipes.
   - Active personal and workspace roles.

9. **One Personal Commerce And Work Home**
   - Buy products.
   - Book services.
   - Order food.
   - Arrange travel.
   - Find funded work.
   - Review Wishes, Signals, credits, earnings and disputes.
   - Switch deliberately into a separate workspace.

### Individual asset

The compounding asset is not a follower count. It is:

```text
trusted matched reach
+ verified demand and outcome history
+ trained permitted Mool Agent
+ outcome recipes
+ settled earnings and goal progress
```

### Individual guarantee boundary

MoolSocial may guarantee only a funded benefit, accepted work payment, price lock or other contractible unit with exact conditions. It does not give every user INR 1,00,000 cash and does not guarantee that a user will earn enough to complete a Mool Wish.

## 4. Shared Workspace Layer — Every Eligible Workspace Receives This

All workspaces receive a profile-specific version of these capabilities:

1. **State My Business Need**
   - Sell a defined quantity.
   - Obtain recurring demand.
   - Fill available capacity.
   - Source an input at an accepted specification and target landed cost.
   - Reserve delivery, labour or service capacity.
   - Recover an eligible receivable.
   - Complete a defined operating or compliance milestone.

2. **Funded Signal Builder**
   - Exact product/service.
   - Quantity and target period.
   - Geography.
   - Authoritative price.
   - Available stock/capacity.
   - Buyer benefit.
   - Direct contributor reward.
   - Total funded cap.
   - Return, cancellation and settlement rule.

3. **Future-Demand Matching**
   - Match only relevant, purpose-consented Future-Need Slots.
   - Do not sell a customer list.
   - Reveal personal information only when permitted and required for fulfilment.

4. **Outcome Contract**
   - Readiness check.
   - Baseline.
   - Target.
   - Evidence.
   - Maximum price.
   - Make-good or refund.
   - Dispute and settlement.

5. **Grow My Business / Earn From Work Separation**
   - **Grow my business:** the workspace funds an outcome.
   - **Earn from work:** the workspace accepts an already funded assignment.
   - The user must never unknowingly cross between payer and earner.

6. **Profile CoreOS And Mool Agent**
   - The CoreOS is adapted to the workspace.
   - The agent may recommend and prepare actions.
   - Money, regulated decisions, legal representations, clinical decisions and irreversible commitments retain required human authority.

7. **Managed Mool Services**
   - Reserved capacity.
   - Managed sourcing.
   - Results workforce.
   - Eligible principal purchase/capacity reservation.
   - Managed back office.

8. **Workspace Mool Node**
   - Demand and supply capacity.
   - Verified operating history.
   - Outcome recipes.
   - Repeat agreements.
   - Reach Power.
   - Proof quality.
   - Earnings, savings and collected money.

### Workspace guarantee boundary

MoolSocial can guarantee only a unit it controls or contractually underwrites:

- an accepted deliverable;
- verified Future-Need Slots;
- funded reservations;
- retained orders under locked readiness and fulfilment conditions;
- accepted delivery;
- collected receivable;
- verified like-for-like saving;
- or a purchase order where MoolSocial deliberately takes principal risk.

MoolSocial must not promise profit, arbitrary incremental revenue, clinical outcomes, unrestricted employment income or organic social reach.

## 6. Direct Reward And Network-Growth Rules

The proposed viral model must remain commerce-based and must not become a pyramid:

1. No joining fee is required to receive basic Purchase Power or funded work.
2. No user earns merely because another person joined.
3. No unlimited upstream/downline commission exists.
4. A buyer may receive a funded benefit.
5. A direct recommender or actual outcome contributor may receive a disclosed reward.
6. A passive ancestor may receive provenance and Reach-Power evidence, but not automatic multilevel income.
7. Every user owns their direct Mool Node performance; no user owns another person's identity or personal data.
8. Cancelled, returned, duplicated or fraudulent commerce reverses only the conditional reward associated with that event.
9. Genuine accepted work compensation must not disappear because the worker did not complete an unrelated Mool Wish.
10. External commercial messages require the appropriate recipient consent and sender controls.

## 7. Proposed Purchase-Power And Credit State Rules

```text
purchase_power_available
-> intent_planned
-> purchase_power_reserved
-> offer_matched
-> sponsor_benefit_reserved
-> contribution_earned_pending_return
-> purchase_completed
-> benefit_applied
-> reward_settled
-> purchase_power_released_or_refreshed
```

Alternative exits:

- user cancels intent -> release Purchase Power; cancel only conditional unearned benefit;
- intent expires -> release Purchase Power; preserve genuine settled earnings;
- business cannot fulfil -> release Purchase Power and apply contracted make-good/refund;
- purchase returns -> reverse event-specific conditional credit;
- fraud/duplicate -> hold, review and resolve with evidence;
- user changes product -> explicitly migrate compatible credits or show why they cannot move.

The UI must never combine these balances:

| Balance | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Purchase Power** | Non-cash planning capacity |
| **Sponsor Benefit Reserved** | Business-funded conditional benefit |
| **Earnings Pending** | Verified outcome awaiting return/fraud period |
| **Settled Earnings** | Payable value through the approved payment route |
| **Purchase Booster** | Extra benefit applied only to an eligible Mool Wish |

## 8. Release And Compliance Boundaries

Before implementation:

1. **Payments and wallet**
   - Do not represent Purchase Power as money.
   - Use a regulated bank/PPI/payment partner where value is stored or usable across independent merchants.
   - Keep sponsor reserves, earnings and MoolSocial operating cash separated in the ledger and settlement architecture.

2. **Direct selling and referral**
   - No multilevel recruitment benefit.
   - Rewards require actual accepted work or completed commerce.
   - Business authorization and product claim approval are required.

3. **Consent and communication**
   - Future-Need Slots are purpose-specific.
   - Users can withdraw or expire their intent.
   - Mool Pulse targets opted-in matching users.
   - Personal contact lists are not silently uploaded or messaged.

4. **Dark-pattern prevention**
   - No false INR 1,00,000 cash representation.
   - No false countdown or scarcity.
   - No hidden expiry.
   - No forced purchase to withdraw genuine earnings.
   - No disguised sponsored Signal.

5. **Food**
   - Applicable FSSAI registration/licensing and operating readiness gate food sales and managed capacity.

6. **Health**
   - No paid patient referral, medicine inducement, prescription generation, clinical decision or clinical-outcome fee.

7. **Mobility**
   - State rules, licences, vehicle, driver/captain, insurance, safety and incident readiness gate activation.

8. **Principal purchase**
   - MoolSocial buys inventory or capacity only under approved capital, title, tax, quality, shelf-life, storage, return and resale policy.
